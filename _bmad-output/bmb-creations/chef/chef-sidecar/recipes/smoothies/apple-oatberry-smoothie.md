# Apple Oatberry Smoothie

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 2
- **Time:** 5 min
- **Category:** smoothies
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** None

## Ingredients
- 1/2 cup unsweetened soy, hemp, or almond milk
- 1 apple, cored
- 1 cup organic strawberries
- 2 tablespoons old-fashioned rolled oats
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1 tablespoon ground chia seeds
- 1 tablespoon pine nuts
- 1 small cucumber

## Instructions
1. Blend all ingredients in a high-powered blender.

## Notes
- Use Mediterranean pine nuts if available for higher protein content.
