# Boston Green Smoothie

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 2
- **Time:** 5 min
- **Category:** smoothies
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Greens

## Ingredients
- 2 ounces Boston lettuce
- 2 cups fresh or frozen pineapple cubes
- 3 kiwis
- 1/2 avocado
- 1 banana

## Instructions
1. Blend all ingredients in a high-powered blender until smooth and creamy.

## Nutrition (per serving)
Calories: 297 | Protein: 6g | Carbs: 57g | Fat: 8.8g | Fiber: 12.3g
