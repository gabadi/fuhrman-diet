# Pomegranate Refresher

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 2
- **Time:** 5 min
- **Category:** smoothies
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** None

## Ingredients
- 1 cup pomegranate juice
- 1 cup strawberries, frozen or fresh organic
- 4 kumquats or 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 1 cup water

## Instructions
1. Blend ingredients together in a high-powered blender.

## Nutrition (per serving)
Calories: 113 | Protein: 0.3g | Carbs: 27g | Fat: 0.1g | Fiber: 1.6g
