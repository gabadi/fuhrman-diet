# High Cruciferous Juice

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 2
- **Time:** 10 min
- **Category:** smoothies
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Greens

## Ingredients
- 3 medium carrots
- 3 cauliflower florets
- 1 apple, cored and cut in quarters
- 6-8 kale leaves
- 2/3 cup watercress with stems
- 2 cups broccoli with stems

## Instructions
1. Run all ingredients through a juicer.

## Notes
- Requires a juicer (not a blender).

## Nutrition (per serving)
Calories: 64 | Protein: 3.5g | Carbs: 14g | Fat: 0.4g | Fiber: 3.9g
