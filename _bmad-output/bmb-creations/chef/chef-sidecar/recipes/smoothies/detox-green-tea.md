# Detox Green Tea

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 4
- **Time:** 15 min
- **Category:** smoothies
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Greens

## Ingredients
- 1 bunch kale
- 2 cups romaine lettuce leaves
- 1 cucumber
- 2 leaves bok choy
- 2 cups unsweetened green tea
- 2 cups frozen raspberries
- 2 cups frozen cherries or strawberries

## Instructions
1. Prepare a green juice by running the kale, romaine lettuce, cucumber, and bok choy through a juicer.
2. Mix the green tea with 2 cups of the green juice.
3. Add to a blender along with frozen raspberries and frozen cherries or strawberries.
4. Process until well blended.

## Notes
- Requires both a juicer and a blender.
