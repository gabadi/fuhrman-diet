# Green Gorilla

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 2
- **Time:** 5 min
- **Category:** smoothies
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Greens

## Ingredients
- 1/2 avocado
- 1 banana
- 5 ounces romaine lettuce
- 5 ounces baby spinach

## Instructions
1. In a food processor or high-powered blender, blend the avocado with the banana.
2. Add the lettuce and spinach.
3. Blend until smooth and creamy.

## Nutrition (per serving)
Calories: 172 | Protein: 5g | Carbs: 24g | Fat: 8.3g | Fiber: 8.8g
