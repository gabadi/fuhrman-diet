# Super Easy Blended Salad

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 1
- **Time:** 5 min
- **Category:** smoothies
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Greens

## Ingredients
- 8 ounces baby greens
- 1 orange, peeled and seeded
- Juice of 1/4 lemon

## Instructions
1. Blend ingredients in a high-powered blender until smooth and creamy.
