# Orange Creamsicle Blended Salad

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 2
- **Time:** 5 min
- **Category:** smoothies
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Greens

## Ingredients
- 1/2 cup unsweetened soy, hemp, or almond milk
- 3 tablespoons blood orange vinegar (or fruit-flavored vinegar)
- 1 large orange, peeled
- 6 ounces romaine lettuce
- 6 ounces spinach
- 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
- 10 whole raw cashews
- 4-5 ice cubes

## Instructions
1. Add all ingredients to a high-powered blender and blend until smooth and creamy.
