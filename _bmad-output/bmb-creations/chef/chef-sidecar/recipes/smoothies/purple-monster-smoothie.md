# Purple Monster Smoothie

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 2
- **Time:** 5 min
- **Category:** smoothies
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Greens

## Ingredients
- 1 banana
- 2 cups fresh or frozen pineapple chunks
- 2 cups frozen blueberries
- 2 heads Boston lettuce
- 1 tablespoon ground chia seeds
- 1/2 cup water

## Instructions
1. Blend all ingredients in a high-powered blender until smooth.
2. Adjust water to achieve desired consistency.

## Nutrition (per serving)
Calories: 233 | Protein: 4g | Carbs: 55g | Fat: 2.9g | Fiber: 9.6g
