# Immunity Power Juice

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 2
- **Time:** 10 min
- **Category:** smoothies
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Greens

## Ingredients
- 4 kale leaves
- 5 carrots, peeled
- 2 stalks bok choy
- 1 apple, cored and cut in quarters
- 1 medium beet, peeled
- Squeeze of lemon

## Instructions
1. Run all ingredients through a juicer.

## Notes
- Requires a juicer (not a blender).
- Contains beets - user dislikes beets, may want to skip or reduce.

## Nutrition (per serving)
Calories: 68 | Protein: 3g | Carbs: 16g | Fat: 0.4g | Fiber: 4.1g
