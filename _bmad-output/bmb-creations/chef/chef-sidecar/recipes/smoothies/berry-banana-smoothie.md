# Berry Banana Smoothie

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 1
- **Time:** 5 min
- **Category:** smoothies
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Greens

## Ingredients
- 1 banana
- 1/2 cup unsweetened soy, almond, or hemp milk
- 1 cup frozen strawberries
- 1 tablespoon ground chia seeds
- 1 cup romaine lettuce or spinach

## Instructions
1. Add all ingredients to a high-powered blender and blend until smooth and creamy.

## Notes
- Frozen blueberries, raspberries, or mixed berries may be substituted for strawberries.
