# Green Velvet Dressing

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 4
- **Time:** 5 min
- **Category:** dressings
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** None

## Ingredients
- 3/4 cup water
- 1/2 cup fresh lemon juice
- 1/2 cup unhulled sesame seeds
- 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
- 1/4 cup chopped fresh dill
- 1/4 cup raw cashews or 2 tablespoons raw cashew butter
- No-salt seasoning blend, adjusted to taste
- 1/2 tablespoon chopped fresh tarragon
- 2 teaspoons Bragg Liquid Aminos or low-sodium soy sauce
- 2 cloves garlic, chopped

## Instructions
1. Blend all ingredients in a high-powered blender until smooth.
