# Banana Walnut Dressing

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 2
- **Time:** 5 min
- **Category:** dressings
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** None

## Ingredients
- 2 bananas
- 2 tablespoons walnuts
- 2 tablespoons raisins
- 1/4 cup fruit-flavored vinegar

## Instructions
1. Blend all ingredients in a high-powered blender or food processor until smooth and creamy.

## Nutrition (per serving)
Calories: 208 | Protein: 3g | Carbs: 41g | Fat: 5.2g | Fiber: 3.9g
