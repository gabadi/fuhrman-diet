# Russian Fig Dressing

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 2
- **Time:** 5 min
- **Category:** dressings
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** None

## Ingredients
- 1/3 cup no-salt-added or low-sodium pasta sauce
- 1/3 cup raw almonds or 3 tablespoons raw almond butter
- 2 tablespoons raw sunflower seeds
- 3 tablespoons black fig vinegar (or fruit-flavored vinegar)
- 1 tablespoon raisins or dried currants

## Instructions
1. Blend all ingredients in a food processor or high-powered blender until smooth.

## Notes
- Double or triple this recipe. Leftovers may be stored and used for three days.
