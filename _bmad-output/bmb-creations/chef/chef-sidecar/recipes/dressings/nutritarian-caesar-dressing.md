# Nutritarian Caesar Dressing

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook (Chef James Rohrbacher)

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 6
- **Time:** 10 min
- **Category:** dressings
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** None

## Ingredients
- 1/2 cup raw cashews
- 6 ounces firm silken tofu
- 3 large cloves garlic, or to taste
- 2 medium celery stalks, chopped
- 1/2 cup water
- 1/4 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
- 1/2 teaspoon low-sodium white miso
- 2 teaspoons Dijon mustard
- 2 pitted Medjool dates or 4 Deglet Noor dates
- 1 teaspoon kelp granules
- 2 tablespoons nutritional yeast
- Freshly ground pepper to taste

## Instructions
1. Combine all ingredients in a high-powered blender.
2. Process until the mixture is the consistency of conventional Caesar dressing.
3. Add more water or soy milk if needed to adjust consistency.
4. Taste and adjust seasonings.
5. Refrigerate until ready to use.

## Notes
- Makes about 2 1/2 cups.
- Contains nutritional yeast - may need substitute or omit in Argentina.
