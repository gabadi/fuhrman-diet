# Almond Balsamic Vinaigrette

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 6
- **Time:** 30 min
- **Category:** dressings
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** None

## Ingredients
- 6 cloves garlic, unpeeled
- 1/2 cup water
- 1/3 cup balsamic vinegar
- 1/4 cup raw almonds or 1/8 cup raw almond butter
- 1/4 cup raisins (reduce to 2 tablespoons for diabetic/weight-loss)
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano
- 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
- 1/2 teaspoon onion powder

## Instructions
1. Preheat the oven to 350Â°F.
2. Roast unpeeled garlic in a small baking dish for about 25 minutes or until soft.
3. When cool, remove the skins.
4. Blend the roasted garlic with the remaining ingredients in a food processor or high-powered blender.

## Notes
- For a Walnut Vinaigrette, substitute walnuts for the almonds.
