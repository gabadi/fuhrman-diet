# Ginger Almond Dressing

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 3
- **Time:** 5 min
- **Category:** dressings
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Onions

## Ingredients
- 1/2 cup raw almonds or 1/4 cup raw almond butter
- 1/4 cup unsweetened soy, hemp, or almond milk
- 1/4 cup water
- 3 tablespoons unhulled sesame seeds
- 3 dates, pitted
- 2 cloves garlic
- 1/2-inch piece fresh ginger, peeled and chopped

## Instructions
1. Blend all ingredients together in a high-powered blender until creamy.
2. Add more water if a thinner dressing is desired.
