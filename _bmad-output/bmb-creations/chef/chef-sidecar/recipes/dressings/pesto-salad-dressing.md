# Pesto Salad Dressing

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 8
- **Time:** 5 min
- **Category:** dressings
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Onions

## Ingredients
- 1 1/2 cups chopped avocado
- 5 tablespoons lemon juice
- 7 cloves garlic
- 4 cups low-sodium vegetable juice
- 1/4 cup pine nuts
- 1/3 cup fresh basil leaves
- No-salt Italian seasoning blend, adjusted to taste

## Instructions
1. Blend all ingredients in a food processor or high-powered blender.

## Nutrition (per serving)
