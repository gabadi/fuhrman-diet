# Ten Thousand Island Dressing

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 4
- **Time:** 10 min
- **Category:** dressings
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Onions

## Ingredients
- 1/2 cup raw cashew butter or 1 cup raw cashews
- 1/2 cup unsweetened soy, almond, or hemp milk
- 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- 1 teaspoon dried dill
- 1 teaspoon onion powder
- 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
- 3 tablespoons tomato paste
- 2 dates, pitted
- 1 cucumber
- 1/4 cup finely chopped onion

## Instructions
1. In a food processor or high-powered blender, blend the cashews, milk, vinegar, lemon juice, dill, onion powder, garlic powder, tomato paste, dates, and cucumber until smooth.
2. Transfer to a small bowl and fold in the finely chopped onion.
