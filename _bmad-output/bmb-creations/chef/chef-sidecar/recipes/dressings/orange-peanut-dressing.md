# Orange Peanut Dressing

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 4
- **Time:** 5 min
- **Category:** dressings
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Onions

## Ingredients
- 2 oranges, peeled and seeded
- 1/4 cup rice vinegar
- 1/8 cup natural peanut butter (no salt added) or 1/4 cup unsalted peanuts
- 1/4 cup raw cashews or 1/8 cup raw cashew butter
- 1 teaspoon Bragg Liquid Aminos or low-sodium soy sauce
- 1/4-inch piece fresh ginger, peeled
- 1/4 clove garlic

## Instructions
1. Blend all ingredients in a high-powered blender until smooth.
