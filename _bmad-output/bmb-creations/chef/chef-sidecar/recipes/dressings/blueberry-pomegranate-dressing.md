# Blueberry Pomegranate Dressing

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 4
- **Time:** 5 min
- **Category:** dressings
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** None

## Ingredients
- 2 cups fresh or frozen blueberries, thawed
- 1/2 cup pomegranate juice
- 1/4 cup raw cashews
- 1/4 cup raw sunflower seeds
- 4 tablespoons wild blueberry vinegar (or fruit-flavored vinegar)

## Instructions
1. Blend all ingredients in a high-powered blender until smooth and creamy.
