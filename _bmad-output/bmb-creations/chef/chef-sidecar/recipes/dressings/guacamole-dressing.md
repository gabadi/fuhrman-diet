# Guacamole Dressing

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 4
- **Time:** 5 min
- **Category:** dressings
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Onions

## Ingredients
- 1 ripe avocado, peeled, pit removed
- 1 medium tomato, chopped
- 1/4 cup chopped green bell pepper
- 2 green onions, chopped
- 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1 tablespoon fresh lime juice
- 1 tablespoon nutritional yeast
- 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
- Cayenne pepper to taste
- 1/4 cup unsweetened soy, almond, or hemp milk (or as needed)

## Instructions
1. Place all ingredients in a food processor or blender and process until mixture is creamy.

## Notes
- Contains nutritional yeast - may need substitute or omit in Argentina.
