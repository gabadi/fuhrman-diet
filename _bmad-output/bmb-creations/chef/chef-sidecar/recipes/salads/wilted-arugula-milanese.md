# Wilted Arugula Milanese

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 4
- **Time:** 15 min
- **Category:** salads
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Greens, Onions, Seeds

## Ingredients
- 2 medium fennel bulbs, cored and cut into 1/4-inch-thick slices
- 1 large red onion, cut into 1/8-inch-thick slices
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1/2 cup orange juice, freshly squeezed
- 5 ounces arugula
- 1/4 cup pine nuts, toasted

## Instructions
1. Heat 1/8 cup water in a large skillet and water-saut√© the fennel and red onion until tender, about 7 to 10 minutes.
2. Add the garlic and additional water, if needed, and cook until garlic is fragrant, about 30 seconds.
3. Add the orange juice and stir to combine.
4. Place arugula in a large, shallow serving bowl.
5. Pour the hot fennel mixture over the arugula and toss to combine and to slightly wilt the arugula.
6. Chop half of the toasted pine nuts and sprinkle over the arugula mixture with the remaining whole pine nuts.
7. Serve immediately.

- Lightly toast pine nuts in a pan over medium heat for 3 minutes, shaking pan frequently
- Use Mediterranean pine nuts if available, or substitute sliced almonds
