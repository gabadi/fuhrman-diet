# Shredded Brussels Sprouts Salad

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 4
- **Time:** 10 min
- **Category:** salads
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Greens

## Ingredients
- 2 cloves garlic, chopped
- 3/4 pound brussels sprouts, cut into 1/8-inch ribbons
- 1/4 cup toasted walnuts, chopped
- 2 tablespoons raisins or currants
- 1 tablespoon nutritional yeast
- Freshly ground black pepper

## Instructions
1. Heat 2 tablespoons water in a large skillet and saut√© garlic for 1 minute.
2. Add shredded brussels sprouts and cook for 2 to 3 minutes, until warm and slightly wilted. Add a small amount of additional water if needed to prevent from sticking.
3. Remove from the heat and toss with chopped toasted walnuts, raisins, and nutritional yeast.
4. Season with black pepper.
