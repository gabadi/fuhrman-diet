# Taco Salad with Guacamole Dressing

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 4
- **Time:** 20 min
- **Category:** salads
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Greens, Beans, Onions

## Ingredients

### For the Dressing:
- 1 ripe avocado, peeled, pit removed
- 1 medium tomato, chopped
- 1/2 cup chopped green bell pepper
- 2 green onions, chopped
- 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1 tablespoon fresh lime juice
- 1 tablespoon nutritional yeast
- 1 teaspoon chili powder
- Cayenne pepper to taste
- 1/4 cup unsweetened soy, almond, or hemp milk, or as needed to adjust consistency

### For the Salad:
- 2 ears of corn, husked, or 1 cup frozen corn, thawed
- 10 ounces romaine lettuce
- 1 red pepper, diced
- 1 green pepper, diced
- 1/4 cup diced red onion
- 1/4 cup cilantro or parsley
- 1 1/2 cups cooked black beans or 1 (15-ounce) can low-sodium or no-salt-added black beans, drained

## Instructions
1. If using fresh corn on the cob, steam for 7 minutes or until tender, drain, cool, and cut kernels off the cob.
2. Blend all dressing ingredients in a powerful blender until smooth.
3. Combine salad ingredients and toss with dressing.
