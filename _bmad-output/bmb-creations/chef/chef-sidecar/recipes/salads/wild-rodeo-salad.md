# Wild Rodeo Salad

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 6
- **Time:** 20 min
- **Category:** salads
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Beans, Onions

## Ingredients
- 6 ears of corn, husked, or 3 cups frozen corn, thawed
- 1 1/2 cups cooked pinto beans or 1 (15-ounce) can no-salt-added or low-sodium pinto beans, drained
- 1 1/2 cups cooked black beans or 1 (15-ounce) can no-salt-added or low-sodium black beans, drained
- 1 red bell pepper, chopped
- 5 scallions, chopped
- 1/2 jalape√±o pepper, seeded and chopped
- 2 limes, juiced
- 3 tablespoons rice vinegar
- 1 teaspoon ground cumin
- 1 tablespoon chili powder
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 teaspoon oregano

## Instructions
1. If using fresh corn on the cob, steam for 7 minutes or until tender, drain, cool, and cut kernels off the cob.
2. Combine beans, corn, pepper, and scallions in a large bowl.
3. In another bowl, combine the remaining ingredients and stir well to blend thoroughly.
4. Pour over bean mixture.
