# Marinated Mushroom Salad

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 4
- **Time:** 30 min
- **Category:** salads
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Greens, Mushrooms

## Ingredients
- 1 pound mushrooms, sliced
- 1/2 cup water
- 1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
- 1/4 cup walnuts
- 1/4 cup raisins
- 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
- 2 cloves garlic
- 2 tablespoons chopped shallot
- 1/4 cup chopped red bell pepper
- 1 tablespoon coarsely chopped fresh thyme or 1 teaspoon dried thyme
- 5 ounces baby spinach, chopped
- 2 ounces arugula

## Instructions
1. Heat a large sauté pan and sauté mushrooms for 2 to 3 minutes until slightly softened.
2. Combine water, vinegar, walnuts, raisins, mustard, and garlic in a food processor or high-powered blender.
3. Mix in shallots, red bell pepper, and thyme.
4. Combine with sautéed mushrooms and let sit for 20 minutes.
5. Serve on top of chopped baby spinach and arugula.
