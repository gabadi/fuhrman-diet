# Beluga Lentil Escabeche

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook (Executive Chef Martin Oswald)

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 4
- **Time:** 130 min (including marinating time)
- **Category:** salads
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Greens, Beans, Seeds

## Ingredients
- 1/2 cup water
- 1/2 cup white balsamic or champagne vinegar
- 1 clove garlic
- 1 tablespoon fennel seeds
- Pinch of chili flakes
- 2 parsnips, peeled and sliced very thinly
- 1 red bell pepper, seeded and sliced
- 1 cup beluga lentils, cooked
- 2 cups arugula
- 1 carrot, diced finely
- 2 tablespoons unsulfured, dried fruit, such as blueberries, cherries, or currants
- 2 tablespoons raw sunflower seeds

## Instructions
1. Combine water, vinegar, garlic, fennel seeds, and chili flakes in a pot and bring to a boil.
2. Take the mixture off the heat and add the thinly sliced parsnip and red bell pepper.
3. Cool at room temperature for 1 hour, then refrigerate and cool for an additional hour.
4. Lay the parsnip and red peppers around a large plate and sprinkle with lentils, arugula, carrot, dried fruit, and sunflower seeds.
