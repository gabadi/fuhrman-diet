# Super Slaw

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 3
- **Time:** 15 min
- **Category:** salads
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Greens

## Ingredients

### For the Dressing:
- 1/2 cup soft tofu
- 1/4 cup soy, almond, or hemp milk
- 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
- 1 tablespoon spicy pecan vinegar (or flavored vinegar)
- 2 dates, pitted
- 2 teaspoons fresh lemon juice
- 1/4 cup chopped pecans, lightly toasted

### For the Slaw:
- 2 cups shredded apples
- 1 cup shredded raw cabbage
- 1 cup shredded raw beets
- 1 cup shredded raw carrots
- 1/2 cup raisins or dried currants (reduce to 1/4 cup for diabetic or weight-loss diets)

## Instructions
1. Blend dressing ingredients in a high-powered blender.
2. Combine slaw ingredients and toss with dressing.
3. Top with toasted pecans.
