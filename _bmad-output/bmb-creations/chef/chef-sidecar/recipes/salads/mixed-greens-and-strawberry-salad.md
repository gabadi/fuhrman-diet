# Mixed Greens and Strawberry Salad

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 3
- **Time:** 15 min
- **Category:** salads
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Greens, Berries, Seeds

## Ingredients

### For the Dressing:
- 1/4 cup raw cashews or 2 tablespoons raw cashew butter
- 1/3 cup unsweetened soy, hemp, or almond milk
- 1 apple, peeled and sliced
- 2 tablespoons dried currants or raisins

### For the Salad:
- 1 head (about 6 cups) romaine lettuce
- 1 head iceberg lettuce
- 5 ounces (about 5 cups) organic baby spinach
- 1 (12-ounce) bag frozen strawberries, defrosted and sliced in half

## Instructions
1. To make dressing, blend cashews or cashew butter with soy milk and apple in a high-powered blender until smooth.
2. Add the currants and blend well.
3. Place the lettuce and spinach leaves on a plate and lay the strawberries on top.
4. Pour the juice from the strawberries over the greens.
5. Drizzle dressing over the greens and berries.
