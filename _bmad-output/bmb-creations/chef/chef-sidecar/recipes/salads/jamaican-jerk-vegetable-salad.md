# Jamaican Jerk Vegetable Salad

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 4
- **Time:** 20 min
- **Category:** salads
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Greens, Onions, Mushrooms

## Ingredients
- 1/8 teaspoon dried thyme
- 1/8 teaspoon dried cinnamon
- 1/8 teaspoon ground allspice
- 1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
- 1/8 teaspoon ground red cayenne pepper
- 2 cups sliced (1/8 inch thick) zucchini
- 1 medium onion, cut into 1/8-inch slices
- 1 large red bell pepper, cut into 1/4 inch slices
- 2 large portobello mushrooms, cut into 1/4-inch slices
- 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
- 2 mangoes, chopped, divided
- 10 ounces mixed baby greens

## Instructions
1. Combine first five spice ingredients in a large ziplock plastic bag.
2. Add zucchini, onion, bell pepper, mushrooms, and vinegar. Seal and shake well to coat.
3. Lightly oil a large nonstick skillet. Add vegetable mixture and saut√© for 5 minutes or until vegetables are tender, adding a small amount of water if necessary to prevent from sticking.
4. Add 1/2 cup of the chopped mango and continue cooking for another 2 minutes. Remove from heat.
5. Serve vegetables on a bed of mixed greens, topped with remaining chopped mango.
