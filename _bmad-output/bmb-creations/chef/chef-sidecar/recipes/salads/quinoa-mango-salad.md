# Quinoa Mango Salad

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 4
- **Time:** 25 min
- **Category:** salads
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Greens, Beans

## Ingredients
- 1 cup dry quinoa
- 1 cucumber, chopped
- 1 ripe mango, peeled and diced, or 2 fresh peaches, pitted and chopped
- 2 pints cherry or grape tomatoes, halved
- 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves, finely chopped
- 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
- 1 1/2 cups cooked garbanzo beans (chickpeas) or 1 (15-ounce) can low-sodium or no-salt-added garbanzo beans, drained
- 5 ounces mixed baby greens

## Instructions
1. Place quinoa in a fine-mesh sieve and rinse under cold water for a few seconds.
2. In a saucepan, bring 2 cups water to a boil. Add quinoa and turn down the heat to low.
3. Cover and simmer gently until all the liquid is absorbed, about 15 minutes.
4. In a large bowl, combine cooked quinoa, cucumber, mango, tomatoes, basil, vinegar, and chickpeas.
5. Serve on a bed of mixed greens.
