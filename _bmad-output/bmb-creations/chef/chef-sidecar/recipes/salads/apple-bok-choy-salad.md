# Apple Bok Choy Salad

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 4
- **Time:** 15 min
- **Category:** salads
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Greens, Onions, Seeds

## Ingredients
- 4 cups finely chopped bok choy
- 1 large apple, shredded
- 1 large carrot, shredded
- 1/2 cup chopped red onion
- 1/2 cup unsweetened soy, hemp, or almond milk
- 1/2 cup raw cashews or 1/4 cup raw cashew butter
- 1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
- 1/4 cup raisins
- 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard

## Instructions
1. Combine bok choy, apple, carrot, and chopped onion in a large bowl.
2. Blend soy milk, cashews, vinegar, raisins, and mustard in a food processor or high-powered blender.
3. Add desired amount to chopped vegetables.
