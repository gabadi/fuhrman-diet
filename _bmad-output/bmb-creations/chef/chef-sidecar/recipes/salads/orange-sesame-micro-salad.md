# Orange Sesame Micro Salad

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 2
- **Time:** 15 min
- **Category:** salads
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Greens, Onions, Seeds

## Ingredients
- 1 fennel bulb, trimmed
- 1 carrot, peeled
- 1 celery stalk
- 1 small head cabbage
- 1 medium red onion
- 1 small head cauliflower
- 1 (3/4-inch) piece daikon radish (optional, for a more spicy salad)
- 1 orange, juiced
- 1 orange, peeled and diced
- 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
- 1/4 cup lightly toasted unhulled sesame seeds

## Instructions
1. Cut vegetables into large pieces.
2. Using the S-blade on a food processor, fill the processor bowl to no more than three-quarters full.
3. Process until the vegetables are the size of large confetti, pulsing several times. Work in batches if necessary.
4. Combine orange juice, diced orange, lemon juice, and sesame seeds and mix with chopped vegetables.

## Nutrition (per serving)
Calories: 311 | Protein: 15g | Carbohydrates: 52g | Total Fat: 9.8g | Fiber: 17.8g

## Notes
- Raw broccoli stalks, green cabbage, beets, bell peppers, and turnips may also be used
- Portion into containers and use for lunch. Salad will keep for several days
- For something different, heat a portion in the microwave for a minute
