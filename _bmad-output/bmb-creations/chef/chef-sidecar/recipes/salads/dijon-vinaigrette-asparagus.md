# Dijon Vinaigrette Asparagus

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 4
- **Time:** 20 min
- **Category:** salads
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Greens, Onions, Seeds

## Ingredients
- 2 pounds asparagus, tough ends removed
- 1/2 cup water
- 1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
- 1/4 cup walnuts
- 1/2 cup raisins (reduce to 1/4 cup for diabetic and weight-loss diets)
- 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
- 2 cloves garlic, pressed
- 2 tablespoons chopped red onion
- 2 tablespoons pine nuts

## Instructions
1. Place asparagus in a large skillet and add 1/2 inch of water. Bring to a boil.
2. Reduce heat. Cover and simmer for 3 to 5 minutes until crisp-tender.
3. Drain asparagus and arrange in a shallow dish.
4. Combine water, vinegar, walnuts, raisins, mustard, and garlic in a food processor or high-powered blender and process until smooth.
5. Stir in red onion and pour over asparagus.
6. Let stand at room temperature for 1 to 2 hours before serving.
7. Sprinkle with pine nuts before serving.
