# Apple Supreme

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 4
- **Time:** 25 min
- **Category:** breakfast
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Seeds

## Ingredients
- 6 apples, cored, peeled, and chopped, divided
- 2 teaspoons cinnamon
- 1/2 cup chopped walnuts
- 3 dates, pitted
- 2 tablespoons ground flax seeds
- 1/4 cup unsweetened soy, almond, or hemp milk
- 1/2 cup raisins
- 1/2 cup old-fashioned oats

## Instructions
1. Preheat the oven to 350Â°F.
2. In a high-powered blender, combine 1 cup of the chopped apples with cinnamon, walnuts, dates, flax seeds, and soy milk.
3. Place remaining chopped apples in a baking dish and cover with blended mixture.
4. Add raisins and mix well.
5. Sprinkle oats on top.
6. Bake for 15 minutes.
