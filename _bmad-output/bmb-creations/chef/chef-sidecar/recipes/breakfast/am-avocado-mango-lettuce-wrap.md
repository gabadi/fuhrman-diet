# AM (Avocado and Mango) Lettuce Wrap

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 2
- **Time:** 10 min
- **Category:** breakfast
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Greens

## Ingredients
- 1 ripe avocado, peeled and pitted
- 1/2 mango, diced
- 1/2 cup peeled and diced cucumber
- 1 tomato, diced
- 1 tablespoon fresh lime juice
- 6 leaves Boston or romaine lettuce

## Instructions
1. Mash avocado in a small bowl.
2. Stir in mango, cucumber, tomato, and lime juice.
3. Spread a dollop of avocado mixture on each lettuce leaf and roll into a wrap.

## Nutrition (per serving)
Calories: 281 | Protein: 8g | Carbohydrates: 36g | Total Fat: 16g | Fiber: 12.9g
