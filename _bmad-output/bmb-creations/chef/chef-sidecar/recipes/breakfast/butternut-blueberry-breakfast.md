# Butternut Blueberry Breakfast

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 4
- **Time:** 20 min
- **Category:** breakfast
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Berries

## Ingredients
- 1 small (about 1 pound) butternut squash, peeled, seeded, and chopped in 1/2-inch cubes
- 2 medium apples, peeled, cored, and cut into pieces
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon nutmeg
- 3/4 cup water
- 2 cups frozen wild blueberries
- 1/2 cup chopped walnuts
- 1/2 cup currants or raisins (reduce to 1/4 cup for diabetic and weight-loss diets)

## Instructions
1. Place squash, apples, cinnamon, nutmeg, and water in a saucepan.
2. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, cover, and cook until tender, about 15 minutes, adding more water if needed.
3. Mash with a potato masher, leaving the mixture chunky.
4. Heat frozen blueberries with walnuts and raisins and stir well.
5. Top mashed butternut squash with blueberry mixture.

## Nutrition (per serving)
Calories: 280 | Protein: 6g | Carbohydrates: 49g | Total Fat: 10.5g | Fiber: 8g
