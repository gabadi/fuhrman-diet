# Polenta Frittata

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 6
- **Time:** 150 min (including cooling time)
- **Category:** breakfast
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Greens

## Ingredients
- 6 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 bunch kale (preferably dinosaur "lacinato", not curly) or collards
- 1 teaspoon oregano
- No-salt Italian seasoning blend, adjusted to taste, or 1 teaspoon Dr. Fuhrman's MatoZest
- 1 teaspoon dry mustard
- 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
- 3 tablespoons nutritional yeast
- 1 cup coarsely ground cornmeal (not instant)

## Instructions
1. Water-saut√© garlic over medium heat 2 to 3 minutes.
2. Add greens, oregano, and MatoZest or other no-salt Italian seasoning blend, and cook 5 to 10 minutes or until greens are wilted.
3. In a separate pot, bring 4 cups of water to a boil. Add dry mustard, pepper, and nutritional yeast, then whisk in cornmeal and continue cooking and whisking 5 more minutes.
4. Reduce the heat to low and stir in the greens. Cook, stirring another 10 minutes (until a wooden spoon can almost stand up in the pot).
5. Spread into a pie pan that has been wiped with a small amount of olive oil and cool for 2 hours to allow it to firm up.
6. Reheat in the oven before serving.
