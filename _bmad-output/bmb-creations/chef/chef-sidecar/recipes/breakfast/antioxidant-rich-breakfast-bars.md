# Antioxidant-Rich Breakfast Bars

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 6
- **Time:** 90 min
- **Category:** breakfast
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Beans, Berries, Seeds

## Ingredients
- 1 cup cooked or canned black beans, low-sodium or no-salt-added
- 1 medium ripe banana
- 1 cup old-fashioned oats
- 1 cup frozen blueberries, thawed
- 1/4 cup raisins
- 1/8 cup pomegranate juice
- 2 tablespoons finely chopped dates
- 1 tablespoon chopped walnuts
- 2 tablespoons goji berries
- 2 tablespoons raw sunflower seeds
- 2 tablespoons ground flax seeds

## Instructions
1. Preheat the oven to 275Â°F.
2. Puree beans in a food processor or high-powered blender.
3. Mash banana in a large bowl.
4. Add pureed beans and remaining ingredients and mix thoroughly.
5. Lightly wipe an 8-inch square baking pan with a small amount of olive oil.
6. Spread mixture into the pan.
7. Bake for 75 minutes.
8. Cool on a wire rack and cut into bars.
9. Refrigerate any leftover bars.
