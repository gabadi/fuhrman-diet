# Butternut Breakfast Soup

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 6
- **Time:** 40 min
- **Category:** breakfast
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Greens, Onions, Seeds

## Ingredients
- 4 cups frozen butternut squash
- 2 medium apples, peeled, seeded, and chopped
- 4 cups (packed) kale, tough stems and center ribs removed and leaves chopped, or frozen kale, chopped
- 2 tablespoons fruit-flavored vinegar or Dr. Fuhrman's Pomegranate Vinegar
- 5 cups carrot juice
- 1 cup chopped onion
- 1/2 cup unsweetened soy, almond, or hemp milk
- 1/2 cup raw cashews
- 1/4 cup hemp seeds
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg

## Instructions
1. Place squash, apples, kale, onion, vinegar, and carrot juice in a soup pot.
2. Bring to a boil, lower heat, cover, and simmer for 30 minutes or until the kale is very tender.
3. Puree half of the soup with the milk, cashews, and hemp seeds in a food processor or high-powered blender.
4. Return blended mixture to soup pot. Add cinnamon and nutmeg.

## Nutrition (per serving)
Calories: 260 | Protein: 8g | Carbohydrates: 49g | Total Fat: 6.6g | Fiber: 6.7g

## Notes
- For a quick breakfast, make this recipe the night before, refrigerate, and then reheat before serving
