# Fruity Breakfast Salad

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 2
- **Time:** 10 min
- **Category:** breakfast
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Greens

## Ingredients
- 1 head Boston lettuce, torn into pieces
- 1 cup thinly sliced fennel bulb
- 1 medium cucumber, peeled, thinly sliced in rounds, and halved
- 1 apple, peeled and sliced
- 1/4 cup chopped walnuts
- 2 apples, peeled and cored (for dressing)
- 1 orange, juiced
- 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon

## Instructions
1. Combine lettuce, fennel, cucumber, apple, and walnuts.
2. Blend remaining apples with orange juice and cinnamon in a high-powered blender until smooth.
3. Pour blended dressing over salad and toss.
