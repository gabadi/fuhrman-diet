# Herbed "Goat Cheese"

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook (Chef James Rohrbacher)

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 8
- **Time:** 20 min (plus overnight soak + 2-4 hours aging)
- **Category:** dips-sauces
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Onions

## Ingredients
- 24 ounces extra firm lite silken tofu, divided
- 1 cup raw cashews, soaked in water overnight
- 1/3 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
- 1 teaspoon white miso
- 1 teaspoon nutritional yeast
- 1/2 teaspoon Bragg Liquid Aminos
- 1 shallot, minced
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped fresh rosemary
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped fresh thyme
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped fresh basil
- 1/2 teaspoon dried tarragon

## Instructions
1. In a food processor, place 18 ounces of the tofu (reserve 1 cup), the cashews (drained), the lemon juice, miso, yeast, and liquid aminos and process until very smooth.
2. Add the remaining tofu and pulse for a few seconds, leaving some texture, similar to fresh goat cheese.
3. Transfer the mixture to a bowl and stir in the minced shallot and herbs.
4. Cover and place in a warm spot in your kitchen to "age," 2 to 4 hours, and then refrigerate.

## Notes
- Makes about 4 cups. Will keep for one week, refrigerated.
- Contains nutritional yeast - may need substitute or omit in Argentina.
