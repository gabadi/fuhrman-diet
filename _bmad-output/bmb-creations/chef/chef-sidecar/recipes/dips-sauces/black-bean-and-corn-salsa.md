# Black Bean and Corn Salsa

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 6
- **Time:** 15 min
- **Category:** dips-sauces
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Beans, Onions

## Ingredients
- 1 1/2 cups cooked black beans or 1 (15-ounce) can no-salt-added black beans, drained
- 1 1/2 cups frozen corn, thawed
- 4 medium fresh tomatoes, chopped
- 1/2 medium green bell pepper, chopped
- 1 small onion, chopped
- 3 large garlic cloves, chopped
- 2 jalapeno peppers, seeded and chopped (adjust to taste)
- 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
- 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
- 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder, or to taste

## Instructions
1. Combine beans and corn in a mixing bowl.
2. Place fresh tomatoes, pepper, onion, garlic, and jalapenos in a food processor and pulse until chopped into small pieces.
3. Add to bean/corn mixture along with remaining ingredients and mix thoroughly.
4. Serve with raw vegetables or healthy tortilla chips.
