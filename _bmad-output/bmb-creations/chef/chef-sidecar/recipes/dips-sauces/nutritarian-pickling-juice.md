# Nutritarian Pickling Juice

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook (Chef James Rohrbacher)

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 4
- **Time:** 15 min (plus 48 hours pickling)
- **Category:** dips-sauces
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Onions

## Ingredients
- 2 cloves garlic, peeled and cut in half
- 1/2 teaspoon coriander seeds
- 1/8 teaspoon whole black peppercorns
- 1 small hot chili (jalapeno, Serrano, etc.) cut in half and seeded
- 1 bay leaf
- 2 cups white wine vinegar or champagne vinegar (1 cup of each works well)
- 1 cup dry white wine
- 1 1/4 cups water

## Instructions
1. Place whatever vegetable or fruit you are pickling in a glass bowl with a lid, along with the halved garlic cloves, the coriander seeds, the halved chili, the peppercorns, and the bay leaf.
2. Put the vinegar, white wine, and water in a small saucepan, bring to a boil, and boil for 1 minute.
3. Remove from the heat, pour over your vegetables or fruits in the glass bowl, and let cool to room temperature.
4. Cover and refrigerate.
5. Your pickled vegetable or fruit will be ready to eat in 48 hours.

## Notes
- All-purpose recipe for pickling. Works well with most vegetables and fruits.
- Enough to cover about 1/2 pound of whatever you are pickling.
- Keeps in the refrigerator for three to four weeks.
