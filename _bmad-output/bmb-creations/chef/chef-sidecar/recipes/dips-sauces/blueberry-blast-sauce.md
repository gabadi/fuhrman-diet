# Blueberry Blast Sauce

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 4
- **Time:** 15 min
- **Category:** dips-sauces
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Onions

## Ingredients
- 2 tablespoons chopped shallots
- 1/2 teaspoon chopped garlic
- 2 cups blueberries, fresh or frozen
- 2 tablespoons wild blueberry vinegar (or fruit-flavored vinegar)
- 1/4 teaspoon thyme
- 1/2 cup chopped walnuts

## Instructions
1. Heat 2 tablespoons of water in a skillet and water-saute shallots and garlic until tender.
2. Add blueberries and cook until soft, about 4 minutes, stirring occasionally. While cooking, partially break up the blueberries with a spoon.
3. Stir in vinegar and thyme and simmer until mixture has a saucy consistency, about 2 minutes.
4. Remove from heat and add chopped walnuts.

## Notes
- Delicious served with steamed asparagus, brussels sprouts, or cabbage.
