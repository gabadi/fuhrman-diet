# Fresh Tomato Salsa

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 6
- **Time:** 10 min
- **Category:** dips-sauces
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Onions

## Ingredients
- 4 fresh tomatoes, chopped
- 1 small red onion, minced
- 2 scallions, minced
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and minced
- 2 tablespoons chopped cilantro
- 2 tablespoons fresh lime or lemon juice

## Instructions
1. In a mixing bowl, stir together all ingredients.
2. Serve immediately or refrigerate in a tightly covered container for up to 3 days.

## Notes
- Makes 2 cups.
