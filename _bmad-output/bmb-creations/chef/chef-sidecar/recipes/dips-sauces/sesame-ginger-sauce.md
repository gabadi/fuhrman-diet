# Sesame Ginger Sauce

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 4
- **Time:** 5 min
- **Category:** dips-sauces
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Onions

## Ingredients
- 1/4 cup water
- 1/4 cup raw tahini
- 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
- 1 teaspoon white miso
- 1 tablespoon finely grated fresh ginger
- 2 dates, pitted
- 1 clove garlic, pressed
- Pinch of hot pepper flakes

## Instructions
1. Blend all the ingredients in a food processor or high-powered blender.
2. Add more water if needed to achieve the desired consistency.

## Notes
- Serve with steamed or water-sauteed vegetables. Goes well with bok choy, asparagus, or kale.
- Tahini availability in Argentina needs verification.
