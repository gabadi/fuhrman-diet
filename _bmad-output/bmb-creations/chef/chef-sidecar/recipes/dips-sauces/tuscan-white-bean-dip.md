# Tuscan White Bean Dip

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 5
- **Time:** 15 min (plus 1-2 hours tomato soaking + 1 hour chill)
- **Category:** dips-sauces
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Beans, Onions

## Ingredients
- 1 1/2 cups cooked great northern beans or 1 (15-ounce) can no-salt-added great northern beans, drained
- 1/4 cup pine nuts
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- No-salt seasoning blend, adjusted to taste
- 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar or black fig vinegar
- 1 tablespoon fresh minced rosemary
- 1/4 cup unsalted, unsulfured dried tomatoes, soaked in lukewarm water until soft (about 1-2 hours), then minced

## Instructions
1. Place all ingredients, except the dried tomatoes, in a high-powered blender or food processor.
2. Process until smooth and creamy. Adjust seasonings to taste.
3. Stir in the dried tomatoes.
4. Chill for 1 hour before serving.
5. Serve topped with an extra drizzle of black fig vinegar and a sprinkle of pine nuts. Enjoy with raw vegetables.

## Notes
- Makes 2 1/2 cups.
- Use unsulfured and unsalted dried tomatoes.
