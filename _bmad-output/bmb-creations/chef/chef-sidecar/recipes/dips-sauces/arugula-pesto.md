# Arugula Pesto

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook (Executive Chef Martin Oswald)

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 6
- **Time:** 10 min
- **Category:** dips-sauces
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Greens

## Ingredients
- 2 cloves garlic
- 1/2 cup walnuts
- 1/4 cup white balsamic vinegar
- 1/2 cup water
- No-salt seasoning blend, adjusted to taste
- 1/2 tablespoon nutritional yeast
- 2 cups arugula
- 2 cups spinach

## Instructions
1. Add the garlic, walnuts, vinegar, water, seasoning blend, and nutritional yeast to a high-powered blender and blend at high speed.
2. Turn the blender to low speed and add the arugula and spinach and blend to a chunky consistency.

## Notes
- Use as a dip or to finish soups or any of your favorite dishes.
- Try with different greens: beet greens, kohlrabi greens, or carrot tops.
- Contains nutritional yeast - may need substitute or omit in Argentina.
