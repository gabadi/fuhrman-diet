# Brown Cremini Gravy

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 8
- **Time:** 20 min
- **Category:** dips-sauces
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Mushrooms, Onions

## Ingredients
- 1 medium onion, thinly sliced
- 6 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 cup sliced cremini or other mushrooms
- 3 tablespoons whole-wheat flour
- 2 teaspoons arrowroot powder or cornstarch
- 2 tablespoons nutritional yeast
- 1 tablespoon Bragg Liquid Aminos
- 2 cups water
- 1/2 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
- 1 tablespoon fresh thyme leaves
- 1/8 teaspoon dried sage
- 1/8 teaspoon black pepper

## Instructions
1. Heat 1/8 cup water in a medium saute pan, add onion, garlic, and mushrooms.
2. Saute until onions are soft and mushrooms have lost their water.
3. Add the flour, arrowroot powder, nutritional yeast, and aminos.
4. Gradually add the water, stirring constantly.
5. While stirring, bring the gravy to a boil and allow it to thicken.
6. Add vinegar, thyme, sage, and black pepper.

## Notes
- Serve hot over baked spaghetti squash or thinly sliced strands of zucchini.
- Contains nutritional yeast - may need substitute or omit in Argentina.
