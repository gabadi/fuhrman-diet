# Mango Pudding

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 3
- **Time:** 130 min (including 2 hours chill)
- **Category:** desserts
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** None

## Ingredients
- 1/4 cup raw almonds
- 2 ripe mangoes, peeled and cut into pieces
- 1 banana
- 3 dates, pitted
- 1/8 cup unsweetened shredded coconut
- 1/2 teaspoon vanilla
- 1/4 cup currants
- 1/8 teaspoon cinnamon

## Instructions
1. Grind almonds in a high-powered blender.
2. Add mangoes, banana, dates, coconut, and vanilla and blend until smooth and creamy.
3. Place in a bowl, stir in currants, and sprinkle with cinnamon.
4. Chill for at least 2 hours before serving.
