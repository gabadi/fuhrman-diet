# Chocolate Cherry Ice Cream

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 2
- **Time:** 10 min
- **Category:** desserts
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** None

## Ingredients
- 1/2 cup vanilla soy, hemp, or almond milk
- 2 tablespoons natural nonalkalized cocoa powder
- 4 pitted dates
- 1 1/2 cups dark sweet cherries, frozen
- 1 vanilla bean pod

## Instructions
1. Split the vanilla bean lengthwise and roll open. Scrape out the paste and seeds inside the pod with a knife or spoon.
2. Blend all ingredients together in a high-powered blender or food processor until smooth and creamy.
3. If using a regular blender, only add half the cherries, blend until smooth, then add remaining cherries and continue to blend.

## Notes
- You can use frozen berries or banana instead of cherries.
- Freeze ripe bananas at least 12 hours in advance.
