# Coconut Carrot Cream Pie

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 8
- **Time:** 90 min (plus overnight marinating)
- **Category:** desserts
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** None

## Ingredients

### For the Filling:
- 1 cup unsulfured dried apples, chopped
- 1/3 cup unsulfured dried apricots
- 1/2 cup muscat or other sweet dessert wine
- 3 apples, grated
- 1/3 cup raisins
- 1/2 cup walnuts
- 2 cups shredded carrot
- 1/2 cup shredded zucchini
- 1/2 cup shredded beet
- 1/2 cup unsweetened shredded coconut
- 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg

### For the Pie Crust:
- 1 cup old-fashioned oats, ground
- 1 cup raw almonds
- 1/2 cup pitted dates
- 1 tablespoon chia seed gel

### For the Topping:
- 2 vanilla beans, split lengthwise
- 1 1/3 cup macadamia nuts
- 1 cup soy, hemp, or almond milk
- 2/3 cup pitted dates

## Instructions
1. To make the filling, marinate the chopped dry apples and apricots in wine overnight in the refrigerator or for 1 hour at room temperature.
2. Combine grated apples, raisins, and walnuts in a food processor or blender, then add in the soaked dried fruit/wine mixture and process until combined.
3. Drain and press excess moisture from carrots, zucchini, and beets and mix into apple mixture along with coconut, cinnamon, and nutmeg.
4. To make the pie crust, place almonds and oats in a food processor and process until very finely ground. Add dates and process until chopped and mixed well. Add chia seed gel and pulse to mix in. Press mixture into a pie plate to form shell.
5. To make the topping, scrape seeds from vanilla bean with a dull knife and add to a food processor or high-powered blender along with nuts, nondairy milk, and dates. Blend until smooth and creamy.
6. Add filling to pie crust and spread on topping. Refrigerate until ready to serve.

## Notes
- To make chia seed gel, mix 3 tablespoons chia seeds in 3/4 cup water and let stand for at least 15 minutes.
- Contains beets - user dislikes beets, may want to reduce or omit.
