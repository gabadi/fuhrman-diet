# Apple Berrynut Bites

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 12
- **Time:** 90 min (including 1 hour soak)
- **Category:** desserts
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Greens

## Ingredients
- 2 cups unsulfured dried apples
- 1 1/2 cups unsweetened vanilla soy, hemp, or almond milk
- 2 cups sliced fresh organic strawberries (or frozen, thawed), divided
- 1/4 cup oatmeal
- 1/2 cup raw pecans
- 1/2 cup raw Brazil nuts
- 3 tablespoons ground chia seeds
- 1 cup organic baby spinach
- 1/4 cup unsweetened shredded coconut, plus extra for garnish
- 1/2 tablespoon cinnamon
- 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
- 3/4 cup dates, pitted

## Instructions
1. Preheat the oven to 300Â°F.
2. Soak dried apples in soy milk for at least 1 hour or overnight.
3. In a high-powered blender, blend soaked apples, soy milk, and half the strawberries with remaining ingredients until smooth. Add more soy milk if needed.
4. Spoon into small muffin cups or small, oven-proof custard cups and bake for 20 minutes.
5. Place 1-2 strawberry slices on top of each and sprinkle with additional coconut.
6. Chill in the refrigerator before serving.
