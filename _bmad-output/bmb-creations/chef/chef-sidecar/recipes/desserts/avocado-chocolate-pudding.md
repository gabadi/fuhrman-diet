# Avocado Chocolate Pudding

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 4
- **Time:** 10 min
- **Category:** desserts
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** None

## Ingredients
- 1 ripe avocado, peeled, pit removed
- 1/2-3/4 cups soy, hemp, or almond milk
- 4 tablespoons natural nonalkalized cocoa powder
- 6-10 dates, pitted (depending on size and sweetness)
- 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

## Instructions
1. Blend all ingredients in a high-powered blender.

## Notes
- This rich and creamy pudding is wonderful topped with a generous helping of fresh berries.
- Start with 1/2 cup milk and add more if needed to blend.
