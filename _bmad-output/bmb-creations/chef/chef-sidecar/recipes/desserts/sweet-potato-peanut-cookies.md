# Sweet Potato Peanut Cookies

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook (Talia Fuhrman)

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 12 cookies
- **Time:** 35 min
- **Category:** desserts
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Beans

## Ingredients
- 2 tablespoons ground chia or flax seeds
- 1 1/2 cups cooked white beans or 1 (15-ounce) can no-salt-added white beans, drained
- 2 tablespoons natural unsalted peanut butter
- 1-2 ripe bananas
- 1/2 cup baked and peeled sweet potato
- 6 Medjool or 12 regular (Deglet Noor) dates, pits removed
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
- 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
- 1 cup whole-wheat flour
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1/2 cup unsulfured dried apricots, sliced into small pieces

## Instructions
1. Preheat the oven to 350Â°F.
2. In a cup or small bowl, mix ground chia seeds with 1/2 cup water and stir. Let sit for 2 minutes to form a gel.
3. In a high-powered blender, combine chia seed gel, white beans, peanut butter, 1 banana, sweet potato, dates, cinnamon, nutmeg, and vanilla extract. Blend until smooth and creamy.
4. In a large bowl, combine the whole-wheat flour and baking powder and mix thoroughly.
5. Add the blended mixture to this dry mixture and mix until flour is totally combined.
6. Stir in apricot slices and thin slices of banana (optional) and mix.
7. Place parchment paper on a large cookie sheet or coat sheet with a thin layer of cooking spray.
8. Drop batter by spoonfuls onto the sheet and bake for 20 minutes.
