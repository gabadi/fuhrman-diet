# Pineapple Strawbana Pie

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 8
- **Time:** 150 min (including 2 hours freeze)
- **Category:** desserts
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** None

## Ingredients

### For the Pie Shell:
- 1 cup almonds
- 1 cup walnuts
- 1/3 cup pitted dates
- 1 apple, cored, peeled, and cut in chunks

### For the Pie Filling:
- 5 bananas (3 sliced and 2 frozen)
- 3 kiwis, sliced
- 1/2 fresh pineapple, chopped
- 6 ounces frozen strawberries, slightly thawed and sliced
- 1/2 cup soy, hemp, or almond milk

## Instructions
1. Make the pie shell by placing the nuts and dates in a food processor and chopping well. Add apple and process until a fine texture. Press into a pie plate to shape a crust.
2. For the pie filling, place the sliced bananas on the crust, pressing slightly. Place kiwis and pineapple on top of bananas.
3. Add frozen bananas, strawberries, and soy milk to a blender and blend until smooth.
4. Pour over fruit, cover, and freeze for at least 2 hours before serving.
5. Remove from freezer before serving to allow the pie to thaw slightly.

## Notes
- Freeze 2 of the bananas at least 12 hours ahead of time.
