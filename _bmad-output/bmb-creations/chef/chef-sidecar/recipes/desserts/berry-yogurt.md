# Berry "Yogurt"

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 2
- **Time:** 10 min
- **Category:** desserts
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** None

## Ingredients
- 2 cups blueberries, blackberries, or strawberries, fresh or frozen
- 3/4 cup soy, almond, or hemp milk
- 2 tablespoons ground flax or chia seeds
- 4 dates, pitted

## Instructions
1. Add all ingredients to a high-powered blender and blend until smooth.
2. Chill before serving.

## Notes
- May be served over fresh or frozen berries.
