# Strawberry Pineapple Sorbet

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 2
- **Time:** 15 min (plus overnight soak)
- **Category:** desserts
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** None

## Ingredients
- 4 slices unsweetened and unsulfured dried pineapple
- 1/2 cup orange juice
- 1 (10-ounce) bag frozen strawberries
- 1 cup sliced fresh organic strawberries, optional

## Instructions
1. Soak dried pineapple in orange juice for a few hours or overnight.
2. Chop dried pineapple, add to a high-powered blender along with frozen strawberries, and blend until smooth.
3. Pour into sorbet glasses and, if desired, top with sliced fresh strawberries.
