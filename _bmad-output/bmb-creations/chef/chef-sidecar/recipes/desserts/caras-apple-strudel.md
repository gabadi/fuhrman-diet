# Cara's Apple Strudel

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 4
- **Time:** 70 min
- **Category:** desserts
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** None

## Ingredients
- 1/2 cup vanilla soy, hemp, or almond milk
- 3/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 3 apples, peeled, cored, and chopped
- 1/4 cup raisins, chopped
- 1/2 cup old-fashioned rolled oats
- 1/4 cup ground raw walnuts
- 2 tablespoons ground flax seeds

## Instructions
1. Preheat the oven to 350Â°F.
2. In a bowl, mix the milk, vanilla, and cinnamon until combined.
3. Stir in the chopped apples, raisins, oats, ground walnuts, and flax seeds.
4. Pour into an 8x8-inch baking dish.
5. Bake uncovered for 1 hour.
