# Red Velvet Sorbet

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 4
- **Time:** 25 min (including freeze time)
- **Category:** desserts
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** None

## Ingredients
- 4 large ripe bananas, frozen
- 10 ounces frozen raspberries
- 2 tablespoons natural nonalkalized cocoa powder
- 4-5 pitted dates, chopped

## Instructions
1. Blend all ingredients in a high-powered blender until creamy.
2. Refreeze briefly (3-5 minutes).
3. If desired, top with Pecan Cream.

## Notes
- Freeze ripe bananas at least 12 hours in advance. To freeze bananas, peel, cut in thirds, and wrap tightly in plastic wrap.
- To make Pecan Cream, blend 2/3 cup pecans, 1/2 cup soy/hemp/almond milk, and 4-5 pitted dates in a high-powered blender.
