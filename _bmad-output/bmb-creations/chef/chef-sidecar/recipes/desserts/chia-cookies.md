# Chia Cookies

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 20 cookies
- **Time:** 150 min (including 1 hour soak)
- **Category:** desserts
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** None

## Ingredients
- 1 cup currants
- 2 cups finely ground rolled oats
- 1/2 cup dried unsweetened shredded coconut
- 1 tablespoon ground chia seeds
- 1 tablespoon whole chia seeds
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 2 tablespoons raw almond butter
- 3/4 cup unsweetened applesauce
- 1 teaspoon vanilla

## Instructions
1. Preheat the oven to 200Â°F.
2. Soak 1/2 cup of the currants in 1/2 cup water for at least 1 hour.
3. Combine the ground oats, coconut, remaining currants, ground and whole chia seeds, and cinnamon in a bowl.
4. Place the almond butter, soaked currants and their soaking water, applesauce, and vanilla in a food processor. Blend until smooth, then add to the dry ingredients and mix well.
5. Form cookies using 2 teaspoons of dough per cookie.
6. Place on a baking sheet lightly wiped with oil or covered with parchment paper.
7. Bake for 90 minutes to 2 hours.

## Notes
- Grind chia seeds in a high-powered blender or coffee grinder. Make a batch and store in the freezer.
- For diabetic, weight-loss, or metabolic syndrome diets, limit serving size to 1 cookie.
