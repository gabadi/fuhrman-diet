# Chocolate Dip

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 10
- **Time:** 10 min
- **Category:** desserts
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Greens

## Ingredients
- 2 cups organic baby spinach
- 1 cup soy, hemp, or almond milk
- 1/2 cup frozen blueberries
- 1/2 cup pitted dates
- 1/2 cup raw almonds
- 3 generous tablespoons natural nonalkalized cocoa powder
- 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

## Instructions
1. Place all ingredients in a high-powered blender.
2. Blend until very smooth and creamy.
3. Serve with a variety of fresh fruit.
