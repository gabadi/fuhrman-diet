# Banana Oat Bars

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 8
- **Time:** 40 min
- **Category:** desserts
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** None

## Ingredients
- 2 cups quick-cooking rolled oats (not instant)
- 1/2 cup shredded coconut
- 1/2 cup raisins or chopped pitted dates
- 1/4 cup chopped walnuts
- 2 large ripe bananas, mashed
- 3/4 cup finely chopped apple
- 2 tablespoons ground flax seeds

## Instructions
1. Preheat the oven to 350Â°F.
2. Mix all the ingredients in a large bowl until well combined.
3. Press into a 9x9-inch baking pan and bake for 30 minutes.
4. Cool on a wire rack. When cool, cut into squares or bars.

## Notes
- For Banana Oat Spice Bars, add: 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon, 1/4 teaspoon allspice, 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves, 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg, 1/8 teaspoon black pepper.
