# Black Bean Turkey Burgers

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 4
- **Time:** 40 min
- **Category:** nonvegan
- **Kosher:** Meat
- **G-BOMBS:** Beans, Greens, Onions

## Ingredients
- 1 1/2 cups cooked black beans or 1 (15-ounce) can no-salt-added black beans
- 8 ounces ground turkey breast
- 1/3 cup no-salt-added tomato sauce
- 10-ounce package frozen chopped spinach, defrosted and excess water squeezed out
- 1/3 cup dry bread crumbs
- 1/3 cup finely chopped onion
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
- 1 teaspoon cumin
- 1 teaspoon chili powder

## Instructions
1. Preheat the oven to 350Â°F.
2. Mash beans with a fork or potato masher.
3. Stir in other ingredients.
4. Form into 4 patties.
5. Bake for 30 minutes or until cooked through, turning once.

## Notes
- Contains turkey - limit animal products to 2 servings per week.
- Use certified organic turkey when possible.
