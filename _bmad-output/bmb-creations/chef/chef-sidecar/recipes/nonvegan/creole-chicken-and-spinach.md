# Creole Chicken and Spinach

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 4
- **Time:** 25 min
- **Category:** nonvegan
- **Kosher:** Meat
- **G-BOMBS:** Greens, Onions

## Ingredients
- 8 ounces boneless, naturally raised, organic chicken breasts, thinly sliced crosswise
- 1 cup chopped celery
- 1 1/2 cups chopped fresh tomatoes or canned diced tomatoes, no-salt-added
- 10 ounces frozen spinach
- 1 large green pepper, chopped
- 1/2 cup chopped onion
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1/2 cup no-salt-added or low-sodium vegetable broth
- 2 tablespoons tomato paste
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh basil or 1 teaspoon dried
- 2 teaspoons chili powder, or more to taste
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley or 1 teaspoon dried
- 1/2 teaspoon dried crushed red pepper, or more to taste
- 2 cups cooked brown rice and/or wild rice

## Instructions
1. Cook chicken strips in a lightly oiled skillet, turning occasionally, for 3-5 minutes until no longer pink.
2. Add remaining ingredients, except for rice, bring to a boil, and reduce heat to medium.
3. Simmer covered for 10 minutes or until vegetables are tender.
4. Serve over rice.

## Notes
- Contains chicken - limit animal products to 2 servings per week.
- Use certified organic chicken.
