# Scrambled Veggies and Eggs

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 2
- **Time:** 15 min
- **Category:** nonvegan
- **Kosher:** Parve (eggs are parve)
- **G-BOMBS:** Mushrooms, Greens, Onions

## Ingredients
- 2 eggs
- 2 tablespoons unsweetened soy, hemp, or almond milk
- 1/2 medium bell pepper, diced
- 2 green onions, chopped
- 1/2 cup diced fresh tomatoes
- 1 cup diced fresh mushrooms
- No-salt seasoning blend, adjusted to taste
- 4 ounces spinach, coarsely chopped, or baby spinach
- Freshly ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions
1. Beat eggs with soy milk.
2. Heat 1/8 cup water in a saute pan and add the peppers, onions, tomatoes, mushrooms, and seasoning blend.
3. Saute until vegetables are tender and most of the water is cooked out.
4. Add spinach to wilt.
5. Add egg mixture and scramble until cooked through.
6. Season with black pepper.

## Notes
- Contains eggs - limit animal products to 2 servings per week.
