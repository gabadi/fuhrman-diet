# Garden Eggs and Tofu with Salsa

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 3
- **Time:** 20 min
- **Category:** nonvegan
- **Kosher:** Parve (eggs are parve)
- **G-BOMBS:** Greens, Onions

## Ingredients
- 1/2 medium onion, diced
- 1 medium zucchini, grated
- 1 carrot, grated
- 2 cups chopped Swiss chard or organic spinach
- 1/2 medium tomato, diced
- 2 cloves garlic, pressed
- 2 teaspoons herbes de Provence
- 1 cup firm tofu
- 1/8 teaspoon curry powder, or more to taste
- 3 eggs, beaten
- 1/4 cup no-salt-added or low-sodium salsa
- 1 ounce nondairy mozzarella-type cheese, grated

## Instructions
1. In a large saute pan, heat 1/8 cup water and water-saute the onion, zucchini, carrot, and Swiss chard for about 2 minutes.
2. Add the tomato, garlic, and herbs. Cook, stirring, for about 2 minutes, until the vegetables begin to soften.
3. Squeeze water out of tofu and crumble. Scatter the tofu over the vegetables and sprinkle with curry powder.
4. Mix with vegetables and cook over high heat until water is cooked away.
5. Reduce the heat to low and stir in the eggs, mixing well so the vegetables and tofu bind with the eggs.
6. Continue stirring until the eggs are cooked.
7. Top with salsa and grated cheese.

## Notes
- Contains eggs - limit animal products to 2 servings per week.
