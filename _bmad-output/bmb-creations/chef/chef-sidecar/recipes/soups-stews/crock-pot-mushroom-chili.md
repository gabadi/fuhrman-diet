# Crock Pot Mushroom Chili

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 4
- **Time:** 420 min (7 hours crock pot)
- **Category:** soups-stews
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Beans, Onions, Mushrooms

## Ingredients
- 1 onion, diced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 green bell pepper, chopped
- 1 cup fresh or frozen corn kernels
- 1 zucchini, diced
- 8 ounces mushrooms, sliced
- 2 cups diced tomatoes, fresh or BPA-free cartons
- 3 cups cooked kidney or pinto beans (or 2 cans low-sodium beans)
- 1/2 cup water
- 2 tablespoons chili powder
- 2 teaspoons cumin
- 1/2 teaspoon oregano
- Dash of cayenne pepper, or to taste

## Instructions
1. Combine all ingredients in a crock pot.
2. Cover and cook on low for 7 hours.
