# Yellow Lentil Dal with Mango

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 4
- **Time:** 35 min
- **Category:** soups-stews
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Greens, Beans, Onions

## Ingredients
- 1 cup dry yellow lentils, rinsed
- 3 cups water
- 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
- 1 medium onion, chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tablespoon minced fresh ginger
- 1 teaspoon ground coriander
- 1 teaspoon ground cumin
- 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
- 2 mangoes, peeled and diced
- 3 cups coarsely chopped spinach
- 1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro

## Instructions
1. Combine lentils, water, and turmeric in a large saucepan.
2. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, partially cover, and cook, stirring occasionally, for 15 minutes.
3. Meanwhile, water-saut√© onion until almost tender, about 3 minutes.
4. Add garlic and ginger and cook for an additional 2 minutes.
5. Add coriander, cumin, and black pepper.
6. Stir the onion mixture and mangoes into the lentils.
7. Return to a simmer and cook, stirring occasionally, until lentils are very tender, about 15 minutes.
8. Stir in spinach and cilantro and heat until spinach is wilted.
