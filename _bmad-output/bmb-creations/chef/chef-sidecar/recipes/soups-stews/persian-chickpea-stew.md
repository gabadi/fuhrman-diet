# Persian Chickpea Stew

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 4
- **Time:** 40 min
- **Category:** soups-stews
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Greens, Beans, Onions

## Ingredients
- 1 large eggplant, chopped into 3/4 inch pieces
- 2 medium red bell peppers, coarsely chopped
- 1 large onion, chopped
- 4 medium zucchini, chopped into 3/4 inch pieces
- 1 cup no-salt-added or low-sodium pasta sauce
- 2-4 cloves garlic, chopped
- 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1/2 teaspoon ground coriander
- 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
- 1 cup carrot juice (1 pound carrots, juiced)
- 1/2 cup water
- 1 1/2 cups cooked garbanzo beans (chickpeas) or 1 can no-salt-added, drained
- 1/3 cup fresh parsley, chopped
- 12 ounces fresh spinach

## Instructions
1. Saut√© eggplant, peppers, and onion in a small amount of water, covered, for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
2. Add zucchini, cover, and cook an additional 3 minutes.
3. Add the pasta sauce, garlic, cinnamon, coriander, turmeric, carrot juice, water, and garbanzo beans.
4. Cover and cook over low heat 25 minutes, or until eggplant is tender.
5. Add half the parsley and simmer 30 seconds.
6. Taste and adjust seasonings.
7. Stir in remaining parsley and spinach. Spinach will wilt in hot stew.
