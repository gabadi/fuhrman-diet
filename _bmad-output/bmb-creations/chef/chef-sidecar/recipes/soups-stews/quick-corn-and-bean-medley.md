# Quick Corn and Bean Medley

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 5
- **Time:** 30 min
- **Category:** soups-stews
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Greens, Beans, Onions

## Ingredients
- 2 cups fresh or frozen corn
- 1 1/2 cups cooked great northern or cannellini beans (or 1 can no-salt-added, drained)
- 1 1/2 cups cooked kidney beans (or 1 can no-salt-added, drained)
- 1 red bell pepper, diced
- 1 medium carrot, diced
- 1 large onion, diced
- 4 cloves garlic, chopped
- 1 medium potato, peeled and diced
- 3 cups water
- 2 tablespoons Dr. Fuhrman's VegiZest or no-salt seasoning blend
- 2 teaspoons dulse
- 1 teaspoon Mrs. Dash no-salt seasoning
- 1 teaspoon herbes de Provence
- 5 ounces baby spinach

## Instructions

### Stovetop Method:
1. Add all ingredients, except spinach, to a soup pot.
2. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 20 minutes or until vegetables are tender.
3. Add spinach and cook for an additional 5 minutes or until the spinach is wilted.

### Crock Pot Method:
1. Place all ingredients, except for spinach, in a crock pot.
2. Cook on low for 8 hours or high for 4 hours.
3. Add the spinach to wilt 30 minutes before done.
