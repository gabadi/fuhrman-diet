# Homemade Vegetable Broth

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 8
- **Time:** 75 min
- **Category:** soups-stews
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Onions, Mushrooms

## Ingredients
- 2 medium onions, chopped
- 1 cup finely chopped scallions
- 3 carrots, chopped
- 4 stalks celery, chopped
- 2 cups mushrooms, chopped
- 2 medium tomatoes, chopped
- 6 cloves garlic, peeled and halved crosswise
- 2 bay leaves
- 1 cup chopped fresh dill
- 1/2 cup chopped fresh parsley
- 8 whole black peppercorns
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano
- 10 cups water

## Instructions
1. Place all ingredients in a large pot and bring to a boil.
2. Reduce heat and simmer for at least 1 hour.
3. Skim and discard any foam from surface.
4. Strain the broth, discard the bay leaves, but reserve the vegetables to eat separately or puree them and use to thicken soups or sauces.
5. Use in soup recipes instead of store-bought vegetable broth.
6. Can be frozen in small containers; will keep frozen up to six months.
