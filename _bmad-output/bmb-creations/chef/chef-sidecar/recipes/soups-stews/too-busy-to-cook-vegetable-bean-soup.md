# Too-Busy-To-Cook Vegetable Bean Soup

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 6
- **Time:** 40 min
- **Category:** soups-stews
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Greens, Beans

## Ingredients
- 3 cups frozen corn, thawed, divided
- 2 cups unsweetened soy, hemp, or almond milk
- 4 cups frozen chopped kale
- 2 cups frozen chopped broccoli
- 2 cups frozen oriental vegetables
- 2 cups carrot juice
- 3 cups cooked beans (or 2 15-ounce cans low-sodium beans)
- 2 tablespoons Dr. Fuhrman's VegiZest or no-salt seasoning blend

## Instructions
1. Blend 1 1/2 cups of the corn with the milk.
2. Pour into a large pot.
3. Add remaining ingredients and bring to a boil.
4. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 35 minutes.
5. To make an extra creamy version, blend half of the cooked soup until creamy and add back to the pot.
