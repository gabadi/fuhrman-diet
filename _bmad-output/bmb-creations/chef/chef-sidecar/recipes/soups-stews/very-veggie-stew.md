# Very Veggie Stew

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook (Talia Fuhrman)

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 6
- **Time:** 60 min
- **Category:** soups-stews
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Beans, Onions, Mushrooms

## Ingredients
- 5 plum tomatoes, quartered
- 3 teaspoons olive oil, divided
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 large white onion, chopped
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon coriander
- 2 zucchini, chopped
- 1 yellow summer squash, chopped
- 1 red bell pepper, chopped
- 2 carrots, peeled and chopped
- 1 cup shiitake mushrooms
- 1 1/2 cups cooked kidney or other beans (or 1 can no-salt-added, drained)
- 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, minced
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
- 1 tablespoon Dr. Fuhrman's MatoZest or no-salt seasoning blend
- 1/2 teaspoon Mrs. Dash or black pepper to taste
- 1/4 cup toasted raw almonds, chopped

## Instructions
1. Preheat the oven to 475Â°F.
2. Place tomatoes on a nonstick baking pan and toss with 1 teaspoon olive oil.
3. Bake for 20 minutes, until tomatoes are soft and beginning to brown. Remove, cool, and chop coarsely. Set aside.
4. In a large pot over low heat, add garlic, onion, 2 teaspoons olive oil, and 1 tablespoon water. Cook until onions are translucent, about 5-8 minutes.
5. Stir in cinnamon and coriander and heat for another few minutes.
6. Add zucchini, yellow squash, red pepper, parsley, carrots, and mushrooms. Cook for 8-10 minutes, adding water as needed.
7. Stir in roasted tomatoes and beans. Continue to cook over medium heat for 20 minutes, checking and stirring often.
8. Add lemon juice, garlic powder, onion powder, MatoZest, and black pepper. Cook for another few minutes.
9. Remove from heat and let sit for 30 minutes.
10. Stir in chopped almonds before serving.
