# Shiitake Watercress Soup

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 4
- **Time:** 30 min
- **Category:** soups-stews
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Greens, Beans, Mushrooms

## Ingredients
- 2 large leeks, white and pale green parts only, washed thoroughly and cut into 1/2-inch slices
- 3 medium carrots, peeled and chopped
- 3 cloves garlic, chopped
- 3 cups shiitake mushrooms, sliced
- 6 cups low-sodium or no-salt-added vegetable broth
- 3 cups cooked white beans (or 2 cans low-sodium, drained)
- 5 cups watercress, stems removed
- 1 teaspoon herbes de Provence
- Black pepper to taste

## Instructions
1. Heat 1/8 cup water in a soup pot.
2. Add leeks, carrot, and garlic and water-saut√© until tender, about 3 minutes.
3. Add mushrooms and cook an additional 3 minutes or until mushroom juices are released.
4. Add vegetable broth, beans, watercress, and herbes de Provence.
5. Simmer for 15 minutes.
6. Ladle half of the soup into a food processor or high-powered blender and puree until smooth.
7. Return to pot.
8. Season with pepper.
