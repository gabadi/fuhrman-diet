# Broccoli Mushroom Bisque

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 4
- **Time:** 30 min
- **Category:** soups-stews
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Greens, Onions, Mushrooms

## Ingredients
- 1 head broccoli, cut into florets
- 8 ounces mushrooms, sliced
- 2 carrots, coarsely chopped
- 1 cup coarsely chopped celery
- 1 onion, chopped
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 teaspoons Mrs. Dash no-salt seasoning blend (or 2 tablespoons VegiZest)
- 2 cups carrot juice (2 pounds carrots, juiced)
- 4 cups water
- 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
- 1/2 cup raw cashews
- 1/2 cup pine nuts (Mediterranean pine nuts preferred)

## Instructions
1. Place all the ingredients, except the cashews and pine nuts, in a soup pot.
2. Cover and simmer for 20 minutes or until the vegetables are just tender.
3. In a food processor or high-powered blender, blend two-thirds of the soup liquid and vegetables with the cashews and pine nuts until smooth and creamy.
4. Return to the pot and reheat before serving.
