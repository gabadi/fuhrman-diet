# Cream of Asparagus Soup

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 4
- **Time:** 20 min
- **Category:** soups-stews
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Greens

## Ingredients
- 1 pound fresh asparagus, chopped
- 2 tablespoons Dr. Fuhrman's VegiZest or no-salt seasoning blend
- 2 cups water
- 1 tablespoon Bragg Liquid Aminos
- 1 cup unsweetened soy, almond, or hemp milk
- 1/4 cup raw cashews
- 1/4 cup raw almonds
- 4 pitted dates
- Chopped fresh cilantro, for garnish

## Instructions
1. Simmer asparagus, VegiZest, water, and Bragg Liquid Aminos in a soup pot until asparagus is tender.
2. Blend asparagus and liquid in a high-powered blender with soy milk, cashews, almonds, and dates, until smooth.
3. Garnish with cilantro before serving.
