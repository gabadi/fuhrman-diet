# Shiitake Portobello Stew

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 4
- **Time:** 30 min
- **Category:** soups-stews
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Onions, Mushrooms

## Ingredients
- 1 large onion, diced into 1/2-inch pieces
- 1 red bell pepper, seeded and thinly sliced
- 2 teaspoons chopped fresh rosemary
- 2 tablespoons Dr. Fuhrman's MatoZest or no-salt seasoning blend
- 1/8 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
- 1 pound portobello mushrooms, sliced 3/8 inch thick
- 1 pound shiitake mushrooms, sliced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 3 tablespoons tomato paste
- 4 cups low-sodium or no-salt-added mushroom or vegetable broth
- 1 tablespoon sherry vinegar
- 2 tablespoons fresh parsley or tarragon, chopped

## Instructions
1. Water-sauté the onion, bell pepper, and rosemary, stirring occasionally until tender, about 8 minutes.
2. Season with MatoZest and red pepper flakes.
3. Add the mushrooms and sauté until tender and juicy, about 5 minutes.
4. Add the garlic, tomato paste, vegetable broth, and sherry vinegar.
5. Simmer over low heat for 15 minutes.
6. Garnish with parsley or tarragon.
