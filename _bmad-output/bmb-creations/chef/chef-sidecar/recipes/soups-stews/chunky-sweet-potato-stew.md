# Chunky Sweet Potato Stew

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 2
- **Time:** 30 min
- **Category:** soups-stews
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Beans, Onions

## Ingredients
- 1 onion, thickly sliced
- 2 large garlic cloves, chopped
- 1 1/2 cups stewed tomatoes with juice
- 1 large sweet potato, peeled and cut into 1/2-inch pieces
- 1 cup cooked garbanzo beans (chickpeas) or white kidney beans (or 1 can no-salt-added, drained)
- 3/4 teaspoon dried rosemary
- 1 medium zucchini, cut into 1/2-inch-thick rounds
- 1 teaspoon Mrs. Dash no-salt seasoning

## Instructions
1. In a sauté pan, heat 2 tablespoons water.
2. Add the onion and water-sauté about 5 minutes, until slightly softened, separating slices into rings.
3. Add garlic and cook 1 minute. Add water as necessary to prevent from scorching.
4. Mix in stewed tomatoes with juice, sweet potato, garbanzo beans, and rosemary.
5. Bring mixture to a simmer, stirring occasionally. Cover and cook 5 minutes.
6. Add zucchini. Cover and cook until sweet potatoes are tender, about 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
7. Season with Mrs. Dash.
