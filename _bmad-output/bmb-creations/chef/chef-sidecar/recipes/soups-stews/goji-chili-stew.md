# Goji Chili Stew

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 6
- **Time:** 35 min
- **Category:** soups-stews
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Greens, Beans, Onions

## Ingredients
- 3 cups diced tomatoes, fresh or BPA-free cartons
- 5 cups chopped fresh broccoli (or 1 pound frozen)
- 1 cup fresh or frozen chopped onions
- 2 1/2 cups corn kernels
- 1/2 cup goji berries
- 2 large zucchini, diced
- 4 ounces chopped mild green chilies
- 4 teaspoons chili powder, or more to taste
- 2 teaspoons cumin
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 1/2 cups cooked pinto beans (or 1 can no-salt-added, drained)
- 1 1/2 cups cooked black beans (or 1 can no-salt-added, drained)
- 1 1/2 cups cooked red kidney beans (or 1 can no-salt-added, drained)

## Instructions
1. Cover and simmer all ingredients, except beans, for 20 minutes.
2. Add beans and cook for an additional 10 minutes.
