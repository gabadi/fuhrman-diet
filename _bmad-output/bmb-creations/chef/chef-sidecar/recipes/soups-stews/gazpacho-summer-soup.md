# Gazpacho Summer Soup

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 3
- **Time:** 15 min (plus 2 hours chill)
- **Category:** soups-stews
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** None (raw vegetables)

## Ingredients
- 1 large cucumber, peeled and sliced into large pieces
- 1 large red bell pepper, seeded and sliced into large pieces
- 1 1/2 cups diced fresh tomatoes (or BPA-free cartons)
- 1 cup low-sodium roasted red peppers in vinegar, drained
- 2 cups no-salt-added or low-sodium tomato juice
- 1 1/2 cups mild or medium no-salt-added or low-sodium salsa
- 1/2 cup fresh cilantro
- 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
- 1 tablespoon Dr. Fuhrman's MatoZest or no-salt seasoning blend
- Additional cucumber slices for garnish

## Instructions
1. Place cucumbers and fresh red bell peppers in a food processor.
2. Pulse until chopped in small pieces.
3. Add tomatoes and roasted red peppers. Pulse again until finely chopped.
4. Add tomato juice, salsa, cilantro, vinegar, and MatoZest. Pulse until well mixed.
5. Cover and chill for at least 2 hours to allow flavors to mingle.
6. Before serving, garnish with cucumber slices.
7. Serve chilled.
