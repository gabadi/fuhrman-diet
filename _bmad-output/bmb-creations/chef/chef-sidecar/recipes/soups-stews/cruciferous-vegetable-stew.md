# Cruciferous Vegetable Stew

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 10
- **Time:** 100 min
- **Category:** soups-stews
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Greens, Beans, Onions, Mushrooms

## Ingredients
- 4 cups water
- 2 1/2 cups carrot juice (2 1/2 pounds carrots, juiced)
- 1/2 cup dried split peas
- 1/2 cup dried lentils
- 1/2 cup adzuki beans, soaked overnight
- 1 bunch kale, tough stems and center ribs removed and leaves coarsely chopped
- 1 bunch collard greens, tough stems and center ribs removed and leaves coarsely chopped
- 1 head broccoli, cut into florets
- 8 ounces shiitake mushrooms, cut in half
- 3 celery stalks, cut into 1-inch pieces
- 3 leeks, coarsely chopped
- 3 carrots, cut into 1-inch pieces
- 3 parsnips, cut into 1-inch pieces
- 3 medium onions, chopped
- 4 medium zucchini, cubed
- 4 cloves garlic, chopped
- 1 (28-ounce) can no-salt-added chopped tomatoes
- 1/4 cup Dr. Fuhrman's VegiZest or no-salt seasoning blend
- 2 tablespoons Mrs. Dash seasoning
- 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
- 1 cup broccoli sprouts

## Instructions
1. Place all the ingredients except the parsley and sprouts in a very large soup pot.
2. Cover and bring to a simmer, cooking until the adzuki beans are tender, about 90 minutes.
3. In a food processor or high-powered blender, blend one-quarter of the soup until smooth.
4. Return to the soup pot and stir in the parsley and broccoli sprouts.
