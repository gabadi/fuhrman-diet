# Black Bean and Butternut Squash Chili

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 5
- **Time:** 30 min
- **Category:** soups-stews
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Greens, Beans, Onions

## Ingredients
- 2 cups chopped onions
- 3 cloves garlic, chopped
- 2 1/2 cups chopped (1/2-inch pieces) butternut squash
- 4 1/2 cups cooked black beans (or 3 cans low-sodium black beans, drained)
- 2 tablespoons chili powder
- 2 teaspoons ground cumin
- 2 1/2 cups low-sodium or no-salt-added vegetable broth
- 1 1/2 cups diced tomatoes, fresh or BPA-free cartons
- 1 bunch Swiss chard, tough stems removed, chopped

## Instructions
1. Add all ingredients, except Swiss chard, to a large pot.
2. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, and simmer, uncovered, until squash is tender, about 20 minutes.
3. Stir in Swiss chard and simmer until chard is tender, about 4 minutes longer.
