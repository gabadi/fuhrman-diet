# Creamy Zucchini Soup

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 4
- **Time:** 35 min
- **Category:** soups-stews
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Greens, Onions

## Ingredients
- 1 large onion, chopped
- 4 cloves garlic, chopped
- 2 pounds zucchini (about 5 medium), chopped
- 1 teaspoon dried basil
- 1 teaspoon dried thyme
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano
- 5 cups no-salt-added or low-sodium vegetable broth
- 1/4 cup raw cashews (or 1/8 cup raw cashew butter)
- 2 cups corn kernels (fresh or frozen)
- 4 cups baby spinach
- 1/4 teaspoon black pepper, or to taste

## Instructions
1. Add onion, garlic, zucchini, basil, thyme, oregano, and vegetable broth to a large soup pot.
2. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, and simmer for 25 minutes or until zucchini is tender.
3. Pour into a food processor or high-powered blender (in batches, if necessary), add the cashews, and blend until smooth and creamy.
4. Return soup to the pot, add corn and baby spinach.
5. Bring to a simmer and cook until spinach is wilted.
6. Add water if needed to adjust consistency.
7. Season with black pepper.
