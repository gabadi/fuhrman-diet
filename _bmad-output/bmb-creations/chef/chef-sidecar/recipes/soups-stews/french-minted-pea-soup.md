# French Minted Pea Soup

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 3
- **Time:** 15 min
- **Category:** soups-stews
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Greens, Onions

## Ingredients
- 10 ounces frozen green peas
- 1 small onion, chopped
- 1 clove garlic, chopped
- 1 bunch fresh mint leaves (save a few for garnish)
- 3 tablespoons Dr. Fuhrman's VegiZest or no-salt seasoning blend
- 3 cups water
- 3 dates, pitted
- 1/2 cup raw cashews (or 1/4 cup raw cashew butter)
- 1/2 tablespoon Spike no-salt seasoning or other no-salt seasoning
- 4 teaspoons fresh lemon juice
- 4 cups shredded romaine lettuce or chopped baby spinach
- 2 tablespoons fresh snipped chives

## Instructions
1. Simmer peas, onions, garlic, mint, and VegiZest in water for about 7 minutes.
2. Pour pea mixture into a high-powered blender or food processor.
3. Add dates, cashews, no-salt seasoning, and lemon juice.
4. Blend until smooth and creamy.
5. Add lettuce or spinach and let it wilt in hot liquid.
6. Pour into bowls and garnish with chives and mint leaves.
