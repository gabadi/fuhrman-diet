# Dr. Fuhrman's Famous Anticancer Soup

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 10
- **Time:** 120 min
- **Category:** soups-stews
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Greens, Beans, Onions, Mushrooms

## Ingredients
- 1 cup dried split peas and beans
- 4 cups water
- 6-10 medium zucchini
- 5 pounds carrots, juiced (5-6 cups juice)
- 2 bunches celery, juiced (2 cups juice)
- 2 tablespoons Dr. Fuhrman's VegiZest
- 1 teaspoon Mrs. Dash no-salt seasoning
- 4 medium onions, chopped
- 3 leek stalks, coarsely chopped
- 2 bunches kale, collard greens, or other greens, tough stems and center ribs removed
- 1 cup raw cashews
- 2 1/2 cups fresh mushrooms (shiitake, cremini, and/or white), chopped

## Instructions
1. Place the split peas, beans, and water in a very large pot over low heat.
2. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, and simmer.
3. Add the zucchini whole to the pot.
4. Add the carrot juice, celery juice, VegiZest, and Mrs. Dash.
5. Put the onions, leeks, and kale in a blender and blend with a little bit of the soup liquid.
6. Pour this mixture into the soup pot.
7. Remove the softened zucchini with tongs and blend them in the blender with the cashews until creamy.
8. Pour this mixture back into the soup pot.
9. Add the mushrooms and continue to simmer the beans until soft, about 2 hours total cooking time.
