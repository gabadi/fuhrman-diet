# Black Forest Cream of Mushroom Soup

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 5
- **Time:** 45 min
- **Category:** soups-stews
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Greens, Beans, Onions, Mushrooms

## Ingredients
- 2 pounds mixed fresh mushrooms (button, shiitake, cremini), sliced 1/4 inch thick
- 2 cloves garlic, minced or pressed
- 2 teaspoons herbes de Provence
- 5 cups carrot juice (5 pounds carrots, juiced)
- 3 cups unsweetened hemp, soy, or almond milk, divided
- 2 carrots, coarsely chopped
- 2 medium onions, chopped
- 1 cup fresh or frozen corn kernels
- 1 cup chopped celery
- 2 leeks, cut in 1/2-inch-thick rounds
- 1/4 cup Dr. Fuhrman's VegiZest or no-salt seasoning blend
- 1 cup raw cashews
- 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh thyme
- 2 teaspoons chopped fresh rosemary
- 3 cups cooked white beans (or 2 cans no-salt-added, drained)
- 5 ounces baby spinach
- 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley, for garnish

## Instructions
1. In a large soup pot, bring the carrot juice, 2 1/2 cups of the milk, carrots, onion, corn, celery, leeks, and VegiZest to a boil.
2. Add the mushrooms, garlic, and herbes de Provence.
3. Reduce the heat and simmer until the vegetables are tender, about 30 minutes.
4. In a food processor or high-powered blender, puree the cashews and remaining 1/2 cup milk.
5. Add half of the soup liquid and vegetables, the lemon juice, thyme, and rosemary. Blend until smooth and creamy.
6. Return the pureed soup mixture to the pot.
7. Add the beans, spinach, and saut√©ed mushrooms.
8. Heat until the spinach is wilted.
9. Garnish with parsley.
