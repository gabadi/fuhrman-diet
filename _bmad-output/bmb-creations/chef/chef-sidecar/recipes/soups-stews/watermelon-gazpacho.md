# Watermelon Gazpacho

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 6
- **Time:** 15 min (plus 1 hour chill)
- **Category:** soups-stews
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** None (raw vegetables/fruit)

## Ingredients
- 1 seedless watermelon, half cubed and half diced
- 5 cucumbers, 3 chopped and 2 diced
- 1 red bell pepper, finely diced
- 1/4 cup minced shallots
- 3 organic limes, juiced and zested
- 1/2 bunch mint, finely chopped

## Instructions
1. Puree cubed watermelon and chopped cucumbers in a blender. Work in batches.
2. Allow to chill at least 1 hour.
3. Add diced watermelon and cucumber to the juice along with red pepper, shallots, lime juice, zest, and mint.
