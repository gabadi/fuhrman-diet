# Bean Enchiladas

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 6
- **Time:** 25 min
- **Category:** fast-food
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Beans, Onions

## Ingredients
- 1 medium green bell pepper, seeded and chopped
- 1/2 cup sliced onion
- 8 ounces no-salt-added or low-sodium tomato sauce, divided
- 2 cups cooked pinto or black beans or canned no-salt-added beans, drained
- 1 cup frozen corn kernels
- 1 tablespoon chili powder
- 1 teaspoon ground cumin
- 1 teaspoon onion powder
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro
- 1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper, or to taste
- 6 corn tortillas

## Instructions
1. Preheat the oven to 375Â°F (optional).
2. Saute the green pepper and onion in 2 tablespoons of the tomato sauce until tender.
3. Stir in the remaining tomato sauce, beans, corn, chili powder, cumin, onion powder, cilantro, and cayenne (if using).
4. Simmer for 5 minutes.
5. Spoon about 1/4 cup of the bean mixture on each tortilla and roll up.
6. Serve as is or bake for 15 minutes.
