# Italian Stuffer

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook (Chef Christine Waltermyer)

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 2
- **Time:** 40 min (including 30 min soak)
- **Category:** fast-food
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Onions

## Ingredients
- 1/4 cup unsulfured, unsalted, dried tomatoes
- 2 cups shredded lettuce
- 1/4 cup chopped parsley
- 1/2 cup finely ground walnuts
- 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
- 1 1/2 tablespoons tomato paste
- Pinch of garlic powder
- Thinly sliced red onion
- 2 (100% whole-grain) pitas or tortillas

## Instructions
1. Soak dried tomatoes, for 30 minutes, in just enough water to cover. Drain, reserving soaking water, and chop.
2. In a bowl, mix soaked tomatoes and soaking water with lettuce, parsley, walnuts, seasoning, tomato paste, and garlic powder.
3. Along with sliced onion, stuff into whole-grain pitas or place on tortillas and roll up.

## Notes
- If desired, 1-2 ounces sliced or chopped oven-baked chicken or turkey breast may be added to each wrap.
