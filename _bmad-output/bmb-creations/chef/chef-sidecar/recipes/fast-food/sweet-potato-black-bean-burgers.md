# Sweet Potato Black Bean Burgers

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 6
- **Time:** 45 min
- **Category:** fast-food
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Beans, Onions

## Ingredients
- 1/3 cup raw cashews
- 1/3 cup raw pecans
- 1 1/2 cups cooked black beans or 1 (15-ounce) can no-salt-added black beans, drained
- 3/4 cup baked sweet potato, mashed
- 1/2 cup diced red onion
- 1/2 jalapeno pepper, deseeded and diced
- 3 tablespoons chopped cilantro
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
- 1 tablespoon cider vinegar
- 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
- 3 tablespoons nutritional yeast
- Small amount of olive oil or olive oil cooking spray

## Instructions
1. Preheat the oven to 350Â°F.
2. Place nuts in a food processor and grind to a fine powder.
3. Add remaining ingredients, except for olive oil, and pulse to combine.
4. Form mixture into 6 burgers.
5. Place on a baking pan that has been rubbed lightly with olive oil or sprayed with cooking spray.
6. Bake for 30 minutes until lightly browned.

## Notes
- Serve with avocado, tomato, and red onion slices.
- Contains nutritional yeast - may need substitute or omit in Argentina.
