# Spiced Sweet Potato Cornbread

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook (Talia Fuhrman)

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 10
- **Time:** 45 min
- **Category:** fast-food
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** None

## Ingredients
- 1 cup cornmeal
- 1 cup oat flour
- 1 tablespoon baking powder
- 12 Medjool dates or 24 regular dates, pitted
- 1/2 cup coconut milk beverage
- 1/2 cup unsweetened shredded coconut
- 1/2 cup baked and peeled sweet potato
- 2 tablespoons ground flax seeds
- 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
- 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
- 1/2 cup frozen corn kernels

## Instructions
1. Preheat the oven to 375Â°F.
2. Combine cornmeal, oat flour, and baking powder in a large bowl. Stir well and set the bowl aside.
3. In a high-powered blender, combine dates, coconut milk beverage, coconut, sweet potato, ground flax seeds, and spices.
4. Combine with the dry ingredients, stirring just until well combined.
5. Add in frozen corn kernels.
6. Pour the batter into a nonstick square baking dish, cast iron skillet, or even bake as individual muffins.
7. Bake for 30 minutes or until the top has a golden tinge.
8. Cool for 10 to 15 minutes before serving.
