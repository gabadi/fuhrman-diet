# Roasted Vegetable Pizza

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 2
- **Time:** 35 min
- **Category:** fast-food
- **Kosher:** Parve (or Dairy if using dairy cheese)
- **G-BOMBS:** Mushrooms, Greens, Onions

## Ingredients
- 2 cups broccoli florets
- 1 large red bell pepper, cut into 1 inch slices
- 1 large portobello mushroom, cut into 1/2-inch slices
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
- 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
- 5 ounces organic baby spinach
- 1/2 cup no-salt-added or low-sodium pasta sauce
- 2 whole-grain tortillas or whole-wheat pita bread
- 2 tablespoons mozzarella-type, nondairy cheese, shredded

## Instructions
1. Preheat the oven to 350Â°F.
2. Toss broccoli, bell peppers, and mushrooms with garlic powder, balsamic vinegar, and oregano.
3. Roast seasoned vegetables on a cookie sheet for 20 minutes, turning occasionally and mounding to keep from drying out.
4. Steam spinach until just wilted.
5. Spread a layer of pasta sauce on tortilla or on top of pita bread, sprinkle lightly with nondairy cheese, and distribute roasted vegetables and spinach on top.
6. Bake for approximately 7 minutes or until cheese is melted.
