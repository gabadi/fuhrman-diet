# Popeye Pitas with Mediterranean Tomato Spread

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 4
- **Time:** 15 min
- **Category:** fast-food
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Beans, Greens, Onions

## Ingredients

### For the Spread:
- 1 cup cooked or canned great northern beans, low-sodium or no-salt-added, drained
- 1/4 cup pine nuts
- 2 tablespoons unsulfured, unsalted, dried tomatoes, minced
- 1 clove garlic
- No-salt seasoning blend, adjusted to taste
- 1 teaspoon balsamic vinegar
- 1/2 teaspoon minced fresh rosemary, if desired

### For the Sandwich:
- 4 whole-wheat pitas
- 1 tomato, sliced
- 1/2 cup thinly sliced red onion
- 2 cups finely chopped arugula
- 2 cups finely chopped spinach
- 1 avocado, sliced

## Instructions
1. Combine spread ingredients in a high-powered blender or food processor until smooth.
2. Toast pitas, slice open a top slit, and spread apart.
3. Stuff with sandwich ingredients and spread.

## Notes
- Use Mediterranean pine nuts if available. Chopped almonds may be substituted.
