# Portobello Veggie Fajitas

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 4
- **Time:** 20 min
- **Category:** fast-food
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Mushrooms, Beans, Onions

## Ingredients
- 4 portobello mushroom caps, cut into strips
- 2 green bell peppers, cut into strips
- 2 small yellow squash, cut into strips
- 1 medium white onion, chopped
- 1/2 Serrano chili, seeded, minced
- 1 garlic clove, chopped
- 1 tablespoon chili powder, or to taste
- 1 cup cooked beans (any type) or canned low-sodium beans, drained
- 3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
- 4 large whole-grain tortillas
- Salsa
- 1 avocado, peeled and sliced

## Instructions
1. Combine mushrooms, green peppers, squash, onion, Serrano chili, garlic, and chili powder.
2. Heat 2 tablespoons water in a skillet and water-saute veggies until tender, about 5 minutes, adding extra water if needed to prevent sticking.
3. Add beans and cook until heated through.
4. Stir in cilantro.
5. Serve on warmed tortillas topped with salsa and sliced avocado.
