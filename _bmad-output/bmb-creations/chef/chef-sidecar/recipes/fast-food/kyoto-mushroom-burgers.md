# Kyoto Mushroom Burgers

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 6-8
- **Time:** 35 min
- **Category:** fast-food
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Mushrooms, Onions

## Ingredients
- 1/2 cup chopped red onion
- 2 cups chopped portobello mushrooms
- 1/2 cup chopped carrots
- 1/2 cup peeled and chopped zucchini
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 teaspoon white miso
- 1 cup cooked brown rice
- 1/2 cup raw sunflower seeds, ground
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil
- 1 cup whole-wheat bread crumbs
- Olive oil (small amount)

## Instructions
1. Preheat the oven to 300Â°F.
2. Heat 1/8 cup water in a large frying pan over medium heat.
3. Water-saute onions, mushrooms, carrots, zucchini, and garlic about 10 minutes or until vegetables are tender, adding more water if needed to prevent sticking.
4. Add miso and blend well.
5. In a food processor, add sauteed vegetables, cooked rice, sunflower seeds, parsley, and basil and pulse several times until well mixed.
6. Add bread crumbs and pulse again until all the ingredients hold together.
7. Rub a baking pan with a small amount of olive oil.
8. Shape mixture into 3 1/2 by 1/2 inch burgers and bake for 10 minutes on each side, until firm.

## Notes
- Makes 6-8 burgers.
