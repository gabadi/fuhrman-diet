# Asian Vegetable Stir-Fry

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 4
- **Time:** 90 min
- **Category:** main-dishes
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Greens, Mushrooms, Onions, Seeds

## Ingredients

### For the Tofu:
- 14 ounces extra-firm tofu, cubed
- 1 teaspoon Bragg Liquid Aminos or low-sodium soy sauce
- 1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
- 2 tablespoons Spike no-salt seasoning
- 1/2 cup brown rice or wild rice
- 1/4 cup unhulled sesame seeds

### For the Sauce:
- 1/4 cup unsulfured, dried apricots, soaked overnight in 1/2 cup water
- 1/4 cup unsalted, natural peanut butter or raw cashew butter
- 2 tablespoons fresh chopped ginger
- 4 cloves garlic, chopped
- No-salt seasoning blend or 1 tablespoon Dr. Fuhrman's VegiZest
- 1/4 cup Black Fig Vinegar
- 1 teaspoon arrowroot powder
- 1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes

### For the Vegetables:
- 2 tablespoons water
- 1 medium onion, cut into wedges
- 4 cups small broccoli florets
- 2 medium carrots, cut diagonally
- 4 medium red bell peppers, seeded and cut into 1-inch squares
- 1 cup sugar snap peas or snow peas
- 2 cups bok choy, cut in bite-size pieces
- 3 cups fresh mushrooms (shiitake, porcini and/or cremini)
- 1 pound fresh spinach
- 1/2 cup raw cashews, coarsely chopped
- 1 1/4 pounds romaine lettuce, shredded

## Instructions
1. Marinate the tofu for 30 minutes in the liquid aminos, red pepper flakes, and Spike.
2. While the tofu marinates, combine rice and 1 1/4 cups water in a saucepan. Bring to a boil, reduce heat and cover. Simmer 30 minutes or until water is absorbed.
3. Preheat the oven to 350°F. Toss the marinated tofu with the sesame seeds.
4. Bake the sesame-coated tofu in a nonstick baking pan for 30 to 40 minutes, until golden.
5. To make the sauce, place the soaked apricots with soaking liquid, peanut butter, ginger, garlic, VegiZest, vinegar, arrowroot powder, and red pepper flakes in a food processor or high-powered blender and blend until smooth.
6. Heat water in a large pan and water-sauté the onion, broccoli, carrots, bell peppers, and peas for 5 minutes.
7. Add the bok choy and mushrooms, cover, and simmer until vegetables are just tender.
8. Add the spinach and toss until wilted.
9. Add the sauce and stir until all the vegetables are glazed and hot, about 1 minute.
10. Mix in the cashews and baked tofu.
11. Serve over the shredded lettuce along with 1/4 cup rice per person.

- Beans or small pieces of chicken breast or shrimp can be substituted
