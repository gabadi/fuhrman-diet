# Brussels Sprouts with Orange and Walnuts

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 4
- **Time:** 30 min
- **Category:** main-dishes
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Greens

## Ingredients
- 1 pound brussels sprouts
- 1/2 cup freshly squeezed orange juice
- 1/4 cup walnuts
- 1 teaspoon organic orange zest
- Freshly ground pepper to taste

## Instructions
1. Place brussels sprouts in a steamer basket over boiling water.
2. Cover and steam about 20 minutes or until tender.
3. Place steamed brussels sprouts and orange juice in a large skillet, bring to a simmer, and cook for 3 minutes.
4. Remove from heat.
5. Toast walnuts in a small skillet over medium heat for 2 to 3 minutes until lightly toasted.
6. Add toasted walnuts and grated orange peel and gently toss.
7. Season with pepper.
