# Mushroom Stroganoff

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 4
- **Time:** 25 min
- **Category:** main-dishes
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Mushrooms, Onions, Seeds

## Ingredients
- 1 medium onion, chopped
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1 pound mushrooms, thinly sliced
- 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon fresh tarragon, chopped, or 1 teaspoon dried tarragon
- 1 tablespoon sweet paprika
- 1 cup low-sodium vegetable broth
- 3 tablespoons tahini (or 3 tablespoons unhulled sesame seeds pureed with 1/4 cup water)

## Instructions
1. In a nonstick skillet, water-saut√© onion and garlic until soft.
2. Add mushrooms and continue cooking until mushrooms soften and lose their moisture.
3. Add lemon juice, tarragon, and paprika and mix well.
4. Blend vegetable broth and tahini. (Heating the broth makes blending easier.)
5. Pour over mushroom mixture and mix well.
6. Simmer until mixture thickens slightly or until desired consistency.

Calories: 123 | Protein: 7g | Carbohydrates: 12g | Total Fat: 7g | Fiber: 2.8g
