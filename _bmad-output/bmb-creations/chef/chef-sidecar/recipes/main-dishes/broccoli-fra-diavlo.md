# Broccoli Fra Diavlo

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 2
- **Time:** 25 min
- **Category:** main-dishes
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Greens, Onions

## Ingredients
- 5 cups fresh broccoli florets
- 4 cloves garlic, chopped
- 1 1/2 cups diced fresh tomatoes or 1 (15-ounce) can no-salt-added diced tomatoes
- 1 cup low-sodium tomato or pasta sauce
- 1-2 teaspoons Italian seasoning
- Dash of dried hot pepper flakes
- 1/4 cup nutritional yeast
- 1/2 teaspoon Spanish paprika

## Instructions
1. Steam broccoli until tender.
2. In a large saucepan over medium heat, saut√© garlic in 1/4 cup water for 3 to 4 minutes.
3. Add tomatoes, tomato sauce, Italian seasoning, and hot pepper flakes to taste.
4. Simmer 10 minutes.
5. Stir in broccoli, nutritional yeast, and Spanish paprika.

Calories: 209 | Protein: 19g | Carbohydrates: 38g | Total Fat: 1.5g | Fiber: 15g
