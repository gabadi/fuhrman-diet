# Channa Saag (Spicy Chickpeas with Spinach)

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 2
- **Time:** 25 min
- **Category:** main-dishes
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Greens, Beans, Onions

## Ingredients
- 1 medium onion, thinly sliced
- 2 garlic cloves, crushed
- 1 inch piece ginger, grated
- 2 medium tomatoes, chopped
- 1 teaspoon ground coriander
- 1 teaspoon garam masala (an Indian spice mixture)
- 1/4 teaspoon ground cardamom
- 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- 10 ounces spinach, chopped (frozen or fresh)
- 1 1/2 cups cooked garbanzo beans (chickpeas) or 1 (15-ounce) can no-salt-added chickpeas, drained
- 1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper, or to taste

## Instructions
1. Heat 2 to 3 tablespoons water in a large pan.
2. Water-saut√© onion, garlic, and ginger until tender.
3. Add the tomatoes, spices, and spinach and cook 5 minutes.
4. Stir in the chickpeas and cayenne pepper and cook for another 5 minutes.
