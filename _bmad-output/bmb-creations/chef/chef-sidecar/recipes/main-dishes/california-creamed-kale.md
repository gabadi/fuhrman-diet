# California Creamed Kale

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 4
- **Time:** 15 min
- **Category:** main-dishes
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Greens, Seeds

## Ingredients
- 2 bunches kale, tough stems and center ribs removed and leaves chopped
- 1 cup raw cashews
- 3/4 cup unsweetened soy, hemp, or almond milk
- 4 tablespoons onion flakes
- No-salt seasoning blend or 1 tablespoon Dr. Fuhrman's VegiZest

## Instructions
1. Place kale in a large steamer pot. Steam 8 minutes.
2. Meanwhile, place remaining ingredients in a high-powered blender and blend until smooth.
3. Place kale in a colander and press with a clean dish towel to remove some of the excess water.
4. In a bowl, coarsely chop and mix kale with the cream sauce.

## Nutrition (per serving)
Calories: 269 | Protein: 12g | Carbohydrates: 25g | Total Fat: 15.9g | Fiber: 3.7g

## Notes
- Sauce may also be used with broccoli, spinach, or other steamed vegetables
- Also delicious mixed with raw chopped red onion and a little tomato sauce on top
