# Sicilian Stuffed Peppers

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 3
- **Time:** 45 min
- **Category:** main-dishes
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Mushrooms, Onions

## Ingredients
- 1/2 cup dry quinoa
- 3 large bell peppers, cut in half lengthwise, seeds and membranes removed
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 medium onion, minced
- 1 medium eggplant, diced
- 1 medium zucchini, diced
- 8 ounces mushrooms, diced
- 2 cups low-sodium tomato sauce
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano
- 2 tablespoons fresh basil

## Instructions
1. Place quinoa in a fine-mesh sieve and rinse under cold water.
2. Preheat the oven to 350°F (optional).
3. In a saucepan, bring 1 cup water to a boil, add quinoa, turn down heat to low, cover, and simmer until liquid is absorbed, about 15 minutes. Set aside.
4. Steam bell peppers, cut side down, over 1/2 inch boiling water until nearly tender, about 8 to 10 minutes.
5. Heat 1/8 cup water and sauté the garlic and onion.
6. Add the eggplant, zucchini, and mushrooms and cook until eggplant and zucchini are soft.
7. Add the cooked quinoa, tomato sauce, oregano, and basil.
8. Spoon vegetable/quinoa mixture into peppers.
9. Serve immediately or bake for 15 minutes if desired.
