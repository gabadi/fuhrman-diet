# Cuban Black Beans

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 6
- **Time:** 35 min
- **Category:** main-dishes
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Beans, Onions

## Ingredients
- 1 cup chopped onion
- 3/4 cup chopped green bell pepper
- 2 cups no-salt-added tomato juice
- 4 1/4 cups cooked black beans or 3 (15-ounce) cans no-salt-added black beans, drained
- 1 1/2 cups chopped tomatoes, fresh or packaged
- 1 cup no-salt-added tomato sauce
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 teaspoon cumin
- 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
- 1 tablespoon red wine vinegar
- 1/4 cup fresh cilantro

## Instructions
1. Heat 1 tablespoon water in a large pan and water-saut√© onions and peppers until tender.
2. Add all remaining ingredients except vinegar and cilantro.
3. Bring to a boil.
4. Cover, reduce heat, and simmer 20 to 25 minutes or until vegetables are tender.
5. Stir in vinegar and cilantro.
