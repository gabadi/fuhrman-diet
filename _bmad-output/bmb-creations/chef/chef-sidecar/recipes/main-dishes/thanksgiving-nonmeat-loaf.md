# Thanksgiving Nonmeat Loaf

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 6
- **Time:** 120 min
- **Category:** main-dishes
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Mushrooms, Onions

## Ingredients
- 2 tablespoons arrowroot powder
- 4 tablespoons water
- 2 teaspoons Bragg Liquid Aminos
- 1 box soft tofu, drained and patted dry
- 3/4 cup chopped walnuts
- 1 1/4 cups chopped onions
- 1/2 cup chopped organic celery
- 2 cups chopped portobello mushrooms
- 1 tablespoon water
- No-salt seasoning blend or 1 tablespoon Dr. Fuhrman's MatoZest
- 1 teaspoon Spike (no salt)
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano
- 2 teaspoons dried basil
- 1 teaspoon dried sage
- 1/2 cup whole-grain bread crumbs
- 2 cups cooked brown rice

## Instructions
1. Preheat the oven to 350°F.
2. Mix arrowroot powder, water, aminos, and tofu together in a high-powered blender.
3. Add walnuts and blend until smooth.
4. Sauté onions, celery, and mushrooms in water with seasonings and herbs until vegetables are soft, stirring occasionally.
5. In a bowl, mix together tofu mixture, vegetables, bread crumbs, and cooked rice.
6. Using a paper towel, spread a small amount of olive oil in a loaf pan.
7. Add mixture to pan and bake for 1 hour and 15 minutes.
8. Let cool for 30 minutes.
9. Turn loaf out and slice.
