# Swiss Chard and Beans Italiano

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 4
- **Time:** 25 min
- **Category:** main-dishes
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Greens, Beans, Onions

## Ingredients
- 1 pound Swiss chard
- 6 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 1/2 cups no-salt-added tomato sauce
- 3 plum tomatoes, chopped
- 1 1/2 cups red kidney beans or 1 (15-ounce) can low-sodium kidney beans, drained
- 1/2 teaspoon no-salt Italian seasoning blend

## Instructions
1. Combine all ingredients in a pot.
2. Simmer on low heat until chard is soft, stirring occasionally.
