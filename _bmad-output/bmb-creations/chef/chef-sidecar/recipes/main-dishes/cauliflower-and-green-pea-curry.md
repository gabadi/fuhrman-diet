# Cauliflower and Green Pea Curry

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 4
- **Time:** 25 min
- **Category:** main-dishes
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Onions

## Ingredients
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 medium onion, chopped
- 1 small carrot, grated
- 2 teaspoons minced fresh ginger
- 1 tablespoon curry powder
- 1 teaspoon ground cumin
- 1 head cauliflower, cut into florets
- 1 1/2 cups chopped tomatoes, fresh or packaged
- 1 cup fresh or frozen green peas
- 1/4 cup water
- 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice

## Instructions
1. Water-sauté garlic, onion, and carrots until tender.
2. Add ginger, curry powder, and cumin and sauté an additional minute.
3. Add cauliflower, tomatoes, peas, and water.
4. Cover and simmer for 7 minutes or until cauliflower is tender, adding more water if needed to adjust consistency.
5. Stir in lemon juice.
