# Vegetable Tagine

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 4
- **Time:** 45 min
- **Category:** main-dishes
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Beans, Onions

## Ingredients
- 1/2 cup water
- 1 large onion, chopped
- 2 carrots, chopped
- 1 red bell pepper, chopped
- 1 zucchini, diced finely
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
- 1 1/2 cups diced tomatoes, fresh or packaged in BPA-free cartons
- 2 cups low-sodium vegetable stock
- 1/2 cup dried apricots, soaked for 20 minutes in enough hot water to cover
- 1/4 cup raisins (reduce to 2 tablespoons for weight-loss diets)
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 1 1/2 cups cooked garbanzo beans or 1 (15-ounce) can low-sodium chickpeas, drained
- 2 tablespoons minced fresh cilantro or parsley

## Instructions
1. Heat water in a saucepan over medium heat.
2. Add onion, carrots, and red pepper. Cover pan and cook for about 5 minutes.
3. Add zucchini, garlic, cinnamon, turmeric, tomatoes, and vegetable stock and bring to a boil.
4. Reduce heat to low and simmer for about 25 minutes, until vegetables are tender.
5. Drain apricots and chop, reserving soaking water.
6. Add apricots, soaking water, raisins, lemon juice, and chickpeas to vegetable mixture and cook 5 more minutes.
7. Stir in cilantro or parsley and serve.
