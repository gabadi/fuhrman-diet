# Mediterranean Bean and Kale Sauté

> Source: Eat to Live Cookbook

## Quick Info
- **Servings:** 4
- **Time:** 30 min
- **Category:** main-dishes
- **Kosher:** Parve
- **G-BOMBS:** Greens, Beans, Mushrooms, Onions

## Ingredients
- 1/2 cup unsulfured, no-salt, no-oil-added dried tomatoes, soaked for 30 minutes in hot water
- 2 bunches kale, tough stems and center ribs removed, chopped
- 1 medium onion, finely chopped
- 1 cup shiitake mushrooms, coarsely chopped
- 3 cloves garlic, pressed
- 1 cup cooked or canned, no-salt-added beans, any type
- 1 1/2 tablespoons sherry vinegar
- 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
- Red pepper flakes to taste
- 1/2 cup no-salt-added pasta sauce
- 1/4 cup Nutritarian "Parmesan"

## Instructions
1. Drain dried tomatoes, reserving soaking water. Chop.
2. Heat tomato-soaking water in a large skillet and sauté the kale, tomatoes, onion, mushrooms, and garlic over medium heat for 5 minutes, adding additional water as needed.
3. Cover and steam for 10 minutes.
4. Add the beans, vinegar, mustard, and red pepper flakes and cook for 3 more minutes or until mushrooms are tender and liquid cooks out.
5. Toss with pasta sauce.
6. Serve topped with "Parmesan" cheese.
